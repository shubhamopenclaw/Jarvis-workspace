## üí° Strategic Findings & Recommendation

### The "Oversized" Trap
The main issue with Canadian distributors (Gildan, etc.) is the **Cut**.
*   **Gildan H000** is a *standard* t-shirt. It is not "Oversized" in the fashion sense (drop shoulder, wider chest, shorter body).
*   **Your India Supplier** is likely cutting these specifically as "Drop Shoulder Oversized."
*   **The Difference:** If you buy H000 here, you are selling a regular t-shirt. If you stick with India, you are selling a "Fashion Fit."

### The "Quality" Reality
*   **Your India Specs:** Ring Spun + Bio Wash + Silicon Wash is a **Premium Finish**.
*   **Local Cheap Options:** Gildan H000 is Ring Spun but lacks the silicon wash (less silky). Alstyle is carded open-end (rough).

### üöÄ Final Verdict
1.  **Stick with India for the 240 GSM / Oversized stock.**
    *   You cannot find a true *Drop Shoulder* 240 GSM blank in Canada for under $10 CAD easily. Your landed cost of ~$9.35 is excellent for that specific fashion cut.
2.  **Consider Testing Gildan Hammer (H000) for "Regular Fit".**
    *   If your "Regular Fit" 200 GSM from India is just a standard tee, you *could* save ~$2-$3 per shirt by buying H000 locally (~$5.50 CAD vs $8.02 Landed).
    *   **Action:** Buy **one** sample H000 from a local shop (or T-shirt.ca sample order) to compare the hand-feel. If it matches your India quality, switch the "Regular" line to local to save on shipping weight and duties.
