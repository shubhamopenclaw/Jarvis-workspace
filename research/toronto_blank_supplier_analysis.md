# Toronto Blank Apparel Analysis for ALPHANIX

## Executive Summary: India vs. Local Sourcing
While your India pricing (~$4.00 CAD) is unbeatable on raw cost, the **Landed Cost** (including $2,000 in shipping/customs/duty) brings your real unit cost to **$9.35 - $11.85 CAD**. 

Sourcing from Toronto-accessible distributors eliminates the $1,950 factory shipping, the 18% duty, and the customs handling fees. This allows you to pay more for a "Premium" blank locally and still hit a similar or better Total Cost per Unit.

## 2. Competitive Deep-Dive

### A. The Gildan Hammer (H000) - "The Value King"
*   **Verdict:** It is significantly better than the standard Gildan 2000 (Ultra Cotton). Since it is **Combed Ring Spun**, it will feel very similar to your India samples.
*   **Pros:** Indestructible, consistent, available for pick-up in GTA to save shipping.
*   **Cons:** Not a true "Oversized" fit. You would need to size up for the ALPHANIX vibe.

### B. Shaka Wear (Max Heavyweight) - "The Streetwear Standard"
*   **Verdict:** This is what most premium "oversized" gym brands use before they custom manufacture.
*   **Pros:** 254 GSM (Heavier than your current 200-240 samples). It has the thick collar and structured drape you see in high-end bodybuilding brands.
*   **Cons:** Higher unit cost, but lower "overhead" compared to importing.

### C. House of Blanks (Scarborough, ON) - "The Luxury Option"
*   **Verdict:** Manufactured right in Scarborough (Roopa Knitting Mills). This is what brands like Supreme and Drakeâ€™s OVO have used for blanks.
*   **Pros:** Made in Canada (Major marketing point for ALPHANIX). 275 GSM is massive quality. 
*   **Cons:** Most expensive. It would squeeze your margins if sold at $38 CAD.
