## üí° The "Clear" Ink Clarification
You asked for "Clear". In screen printing, this usually means **Curable Reducer**.
*   **Reducer:** Thins the ink, makes the print feel softer (less "bulletproof vest" feeling). **BUY THIS.**
*   **Extender Base:** Makes ink more transparent (for vintage look).
*   **Gel Clear:** For glossy 3D effects. (Likely not what you want for Institutional vibe).

**My Advice:** Go to **Screentec**. Being able to ask "Hey, which White is curing best right now?" face-to-face is worth the extra $20. Plus, no shipping damage.
