## 2026-02-23 (Field Trend Scan: T-Shirts)

### üëï Design Constraints (T-Shirts)
- **Focus:** Round Neck Oversize & Regular Fit.
- **Vibe:** "Institutional" Minimalist.

### üìç Placement Trends
- **Offset Tech:** Moving away from center-chest. High-value placement is now **Left Clavicle (Collarbone)** for small text, or **Vertical Spine** alignment for back text.
- **Side-Rib:** Vertical text placements along the ribcage/side seam are rising.
- **Sleeve Detail:** Text wrapped around the bicep hem is rising (accentuating the pump).

### üî† Typography
- **Style:** **Expanded Sans-Serif** (Technical/Sci-Fi look) is dominating the high-end "Institutional" space.
- **Alternative:** "Human-Imperfect" Serif. Hand-drawn or distorted type that contrasts with the technical fabric.
- **Spacing:** Wide tracking (e.g., A L P H A N I X).
- **Contrast:** Tiny technical text vs. Massive back statement.

### ‚öñÔ∏è Logo-to-Text Ratio
- **Verdict:** **Text-Heavy / Spec-Heavy.**
- **Trend:** "Apparel as Equipment." Designs that look like blueprints or technical spec sheets (fabric weight, manufacturing location, "use case") are outperforming standard logo tees.

- **Back:** Massive, blurred/distorted "ALPHANIX" centered, overlaid with sharp, crisp coordinates of the gym/city.
