# Consolidated Design Summaries - ALPHANIX

## [15-03-26] Drop: Fluidity & Structural Inversion

### Design 051: THE LIQUID GRADIENT WRAP
- **Vibe**: Liquid metal meets organic flow. Moving away from rigid geometry into fluid, natural-yet-digital shapes.
- **Placement**: Lower Left Hip to Back-Right Wrap.
- **Ink**: Deep Ultraviolet to Electric Cobalt Gradient (Split-fountain).
- **Gemini Prompt (Studio Shot)**: Studio photography, flat lay of a 300 GSM Matte Black t-shirt. A large, organic "liquid spill" graphic wraps from the lower left hip to the back-right side. The graphic features a smooth Deep Ultraviolet to Electric Cobalt gradient with a high-gloss "wet look" finish. The ALPHANIX logo is distorted within the fluid shape. Sharp lighting, 8k resolution, hyper-modern aesthetic.

### Design 052: THE NEGATIVE ARCHITECT
- **Vibe**: Power through absence. Brutalist, structural, deconstructed.
- **Placement**: Vertical Centered Spine (Nape to Mid-Back).
- **Ink**: Matte Safety Orange (Negative space cutout).
- **Gemini Prompt (Studio Shot)**: Professional studio photograph, back view of a model in a Charcoal Grey heavyweight t-shirt. A long, narrow vertical block of Matte Safety Orange ink runs down the center spine. The word 'ALPHANIX' is visible as a negative space cutout, showing the charcoal fabric underneath. High-contrast lighting, 85mm lens, industrial brutalist vibe.
