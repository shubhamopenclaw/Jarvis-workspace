# ALPHANIX Design History
This file tracks all generated design concepts to prevent repetition and ensure brand evolution.

## 2026-02-22 (Initial Concepts)

### Concept 1: Regular Fit (The "Discipline" Series)
**Text:** "EVERY SET COUNTS" (Small, Sans-Serif, Tracking 200)
**Placement:** Center chest, high placement (clavicle line).
**Typography:** Clean, geometric sans-serif (e.g., Montserrat or Futura Medium).
**Color:** Tone-on-tone (Black on Black, or Smoke on Dark Gray).
**Vibe:** Quiet confidence. The work is done in silence. No screaming.
**Micro-Detail:** Small ALPHANIX logo (1 inch) at the nape of the neck (back).

### Concept 2: Oversized Fit (The "Monument" Series)
**Text:** "MMXXVI" (Roman Numerals for 2026 - Founding Year)
**Placement:** Massive back print, vertical spine alignment or shoulder-to-shoulder (top back).
**Typography:** Bold, heavy serif or block sans-serif (e.g., Oswald Bold).
**Color:** Off-white/Cream on Black/Denim Blue.
**Vibe:** Legacy. Building something that lasts. Essentials/Fear of God architectural feel.
**Micro-Detail:** "ALPHANIX / EST. 2026" in tiny text (10pt) under the main graphic.
