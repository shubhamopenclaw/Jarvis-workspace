# Design Concept: ALPHANIX Design-028-070326-19-00

## 1. LOUD Concept: "ARCHITECTURAL EROSION"
**The Vibe:** High-impact technical data combined with eroded typography. A "glitch in the institution" aesthetic.

### Technical Breakdown
*   **Placement:** Large Back Print (Offset Left)
*   **Max Dimensions:** 14" x 16"
*   **Ink:** White Plastisol (High Opacity)
*   **Design Description:** The word "ALPHANIX" in a massive, condensed bold display font (Bebas Neue style) running vertically down the left side of the back. The letters are partially "eroded" or sliced by horizontal wireframe lines. Overlapping this, in the center, is a small block of technical text: "SECTOR: T-DOT // OPS_ID: 416-647 // INSTITUTIONAL ATHLETICISM."

### Prompt 1: The "Studio" Shot (Verification)
A hyper-realistic professional studio photograph of the ALPHANIX Primary Model Identity wearing a premium heavyweight oversized cotton t-shirt in Black colour. The model stands confidently with his back turned slightly toward the camera against a soft off-white seamless studio background with subtle gradient shadow. High-end commercial fitness lighting with soft overhead key light and controlled rim light outlining the shoulders and traps. The graphic design features the word "ALPHANIX" in a massive, condensed bold display font running vertically down the left side of the back, partially eroded by horizontal wireframe lines, with a central block of technical text "SECTOR: T-DOT // OPS_ID: 416-647" printed in thick white colour using matte screen-print ink. Allow only natural fabric folds. Shot on an 85mm lens, photorealistic, sharp focus, clean editorial campaign aesthetic, rugged luxury fitness brand.

### Prompt 2: The "Atmospheric" Shot (Vibe)
A cinematic editorial photograph of a premium heavyweight oversized t-shirt in Black colour hanging naturally from a matte black wooden hanger on a tree branch outdoors. The graphic design features the word "ALPHANIX" in a massive, condensed bold display font running vertically down the left side of the back, partially eroded by horizontal wireframe lines, with a central block of technical text "SECTOR: T-DOT // OPS_ID: 416-647" printed with a matte white plastisol screen-printing ink. The Toronto skyline is visible in the background at sunrise, softly out of focus with shallow depth of field (85mm lens look). Early morning warm natural light casts subtle highlights and realistic shadows on the fabric. Slight breeze movement creates natural cotton drape. Photorealistic, high-end fashion campaign aesthetic, minimal color grading, rugged luxury mood.

## 2. QUIET Concept: "THE MONOLITH"
**The Vibe:** Pure minimalism. Focus on geometric discipline and premium placement.

### Technical Breakdown
*   **Placement:** Right Rib / Side Seam (Vertical)
*   **Max Dimensions:** 2" x 8"
*   **Ink:** Grey Plastisol (Subtle Contrast on Black)
*   **Design Description:** A single vertical line of geometric sans-serif text reading ".ALPHANIX / EST. MMXXVI". The text has extreme kerning (letter spacing). Above the text is a small, hollow wireframe square (0.5" x 0.5").
