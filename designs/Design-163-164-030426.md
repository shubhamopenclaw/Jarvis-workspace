# [03-04-26] Drop: Neo-Grunge Distortion & Utility-Grip

## Design-163: THE DISTORTED NEO-GRUNGE MANTLE
- **Vibe**: 2026 "Gym-to-Street" / Neo-Grunge. A raw, high-energy evolution of the 90s aesthetic mixed with digital precision. Focuses on an "eroding" mantle that frames the upper body.
- **Placement**: Massive Asymmetrical 360-degree "Eroded Mantle" (Left Shoulder-Cap down to Center Chest and wrapping to Right Scapula).
- **Ink**: Industrial Charcoal (Heavy-Crackle) + Electric Cyan (Digital-Spray Halftone) + 3D Clear "Liquid Silicone" (Drip Accents).
- **Gemini Prompt (Studio Shot)**: Front-diagonal dynamic studio photograph of a 'Void Black' 240 GSM luxury oversized t-shirt. A massive 'Eroded Mantle' graphic in 'Industrial Charcoal' (Heavy-Crackle) and 'Electric Cyan' digital spray mapping the collar, left shoulder, and chest. The 'ALPHANIX' wordmark is partially obscured by the crackle texture, with 3D clear 'Liquid Silicone' drips at the intersections. 8k.

## Design-164: THE KINETIC UTILITY-GRIP
- **Vibe**: 2026 High-Tech Performance / Utility-Tactile. Inspired by performance footwear outsoles and tactical grip patterns, creating a functional, aggressive frame for the torso.
- **Placement**: Symmetrical Lateral "Grip-Panels" (Mid-Rib to Bottom Hem) + Right Forearm "Pulse-Block".
- **Ink**: Matte Obsidian (3mm High-Density Silicone) + Neon Solar-Yellow (Micro-Typography) + Sand-Grain Texture.
- **Gemini Prompt (Studio Shot)**: Side-profile minimalist studio photograph of a 'Stone Grey' 300 GSM heavyweight boxy t-shirt. Raised 3mm 'Matte Obsidian' high-density silicone 'Grip-Panels' with a visible sand-grain texture mapping the side ribs. The word 'ALPHANIX' is vertically embossed into the silicone in 'Neon Solar-Yellow' micro-typography. 8k.
