## 1. LOUD Concept: "The Concrete Monument"
**Type:** Statement / "The Monument"
**Placement:** Full Back (Max 14"x18")
**Vibe:** Brutalist, Architectural, Power. Merging the city's icon with the gym's tool.

### Visual Description
A massive, vertical composition dominated by a high-contrast, brutalist interpretation of the CN Tower. The tower's needle extends sharply upwards. The base of the tower morphs seamlessly into the knurled handle of a heavy barbell, grounding the architecture in physical effort. Surrounding the tower is a circular text lockup: "ALPHANIX ATHLETIC CLUB" (Top arc) and "EST. MMXXVI" (Bottom arc) in a bold, condensed serif font (reminiscent of university stone engravings). The entire graphic has a slight "photocopy" or "halftone" texture to ensure it prints well as a single-color screen print.

### Gemini Prompt (Optimized for nano-banana-pro)
> **Role:** Professional Streetwear Graphic Designer.
> **Task:** Create a screen-print ready vector graphic for a t-shirt back.
> **Subject:** A fusion of the CN Tower and a barbell.
> **Style:** Brutalist architecture, high-contrast black and white, flat vector, wireframe aesthetic.
> **Details:**
> - Central element: The CN Tower silhouette, vertically stretched, transitioning into a barbell texture at the bottom.
> - Typography: Arched text above "ALPHANIX ATHLETIC CLUB" in a bold, condensed serif font (Bodoni/Didot style but heavier).
> - Texture: Clean lines with slight distressing/grain for a vintage athletic feel.
> - Constraint: Single color (white ink on black background). No gradients.
> - Composition: Vertical, centered, commanding.
> **Output:** A flat, 2D design suitable for plastisol screen printing.

**Type:** Everyday / "The Coordinate"
**Placement:** Center Chest (Small - approx 4" wide)

### Visual Description

The word "ALPHANIX" appears in a medium-weight Montserrat (clean sans-serif), tracked out significantly (wide letter-spacing) to create a sense of breath and luxury. The entire block is compact, centered on the chest, creating a "uniform" or "issued gear" look.
