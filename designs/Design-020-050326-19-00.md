# Design Concept: ALPHANIX 020 (05/03/26)

## Concept 1: "DISCIPLINE OVERRIDE" (LOUD)
**The Vibe:** Heavy, bold typographic dominance with a technical/industrial edge.
- **Placement:** Full Back (14" x 16")
- **Ink:** White Plastisol (High Opacity)
- **Description:** Large, bold condensed sans-serif "ALPHANIX" across the shoulders. Below it, a large "01" in a wireframe/eroded texture. Overlaid on the "01" is a vertical tech-spec block reading "SECTOR: 6IX / STATUS: ACTIVE / PROTOCOL: DISCIPLINE" in small, crisp geometric font.

### Prompt 1: The "Studio" Shot (Verification)
A hyper-realistic professional studio photograph of the ALPHANIX Primary Model Identity wearing a premium heavyweight oversized cotton t-shirt in Black colour. The model is turned away, showing the back of the shirt. He stands confidently against a soft off-white seamless studio background with subtle gradient shadow. High-end commercial fitness lighting with soft overhead key light and controlled rim light outlining the shoulders and traps. The graphic features a large, bold condensed sans-serif "ALPHANIX" printed across the upper back in thick white colour. Below it is a large "01" with a subtle eroded wireframe texture, overlaid with a vertical technical text block reading "SECTOR: 6IX / STATUS: ACTIVE / PROTOCOL: DISCIPLINE" in small, crisp geometric font. Thick matte screen-print ink. Allow only natural fabric folds. Shot on an 85mm lens, photorealistic, sharp focus, clean editorial campaign aesthetic, rugged luxury fitness brand.

### Prompt 2: The "Atmospheric" Shot (Vibe)
A cinematic editorial photograph of a premium heavyweight oversized t-shirt in Black colour hanging naturally from a matte black wooden hanger on a tree branch outdoors. The graphic features a bold "ALPHANIX" typography across the back shoulders and a large "01" with industrial tech-spec text overlays, printed with a matte white plastisol screen-printing ink. The Toronto skyline is visible in the background at sunrise, softly out of focus with shallow depth of field (85mm lens look). Early morning warm natural light casts subtle highlights and realistic shadows on the fabric. Slight breeze movement creates natural cotton drape. Photorealistic, high-end fashion campaign aesthetic, minimal color grading, rugged luxury mood.

## Concept 2: "CORE ESSENCE" (QUIET)
**The Vibe:** Minimalist, high-end, asymmetric balance.
- **Placement:** Left Chest (Small) + Nape of Neck (Tiny)
- **Ink:** Matte Grey Plastisol on Black Shirt
- **Description:** A small 2" x 2" square on the left chest containing the "ALPHANIX" text in ultra-wide kerning, justified to the bottom-right. On the back, exactly at the nape of the neck, a single "6IX" tech-mark in a 0.5" font.

### Prompt 1: The "Studio" Shot (Verification)
A hyper-realistic professional studio photograph of the ALPHANIX Primary Model Identity wearing a premium heavyweight oversized cotton t-shirt in Black colour. The model stands confidently in a 3/4 side profile against a soft off-white seamless studio background. High-end commercial fitness lighting with soft overhead key light. The graphic is a minimalist 2-inch matte grey square on the left chest containing "ALPHANIX" in a wide-spaced geometric font justified to the bottom-right. The texture of the matte grey plastisol ink is visible on the black cotton. Allow only natural fabric folds. Shot on an 85mm lens, photorealistic, sharp focus, clean editorial campaign aesthetic, rugged luxury fitness brand.

### Prompt 2: The "Atmospheric" Shot (Vibe)
A cinematic editorial photograph of a premium heavyweight oversized t-shirt in Black colour hanging naturally from a matte black wooden hanger on a tree branch outdoors. The minimalist chest graphic is a subtle matte grey square with wide-spaced "ALPHANIX" typography, printed with a matte plastisol screen-printing ink. The Toronto skyline is visible in the background at sunrise, softly out of focus with shallow depth of field (85mm lens look). Early morning warm natural light casts subtle highlights and realistic shadows on the fabric. Slight breeze movement creates natural cotton drape. Photorealistic, high-end fashion campaign aesthetic, minimal color grading, rugged luxury mood.
