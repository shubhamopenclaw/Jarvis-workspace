## Design-055: "The Primary Sculptor"
*   **Name:** The Primary Sculptor
*   **Design Intent/Rationale:** Directly targets the 2026 trend of "primary colors" (specifically Primary Red) and "contrast piping/seam detailing that sculpts." Instead of literal seams, we use a bold, wrap-around geometric "cage" that follows the shoulder-to-chest anatomy, making the garment look structurally reinforced. 
*   **Technical Breakdown:**
    *   **Placement:** Dual Shoulder to Chest Wrap (Asymmetrical "Harness" look)
    *   **Ink:** High-Density Matte White (3D texture)
    *   **Garment:** Primary Red Heavyweight Cotton (300 GSM)
*   **Gemini Studio Prompt:**
    `A hyper-realistic professional studio photograph of a premium 300 GSM heavyweight t-shirt in Vibrant Primary Red. The shirt features an asymmetrical 'structural cage' graphic in high-density matte white ink that wraps over the left shoulder and curves across the chest, mimicking technical garment reinforcement. The word 'ALPHANIX' is embedded into the thickest line on the chest in a clean, sans-serif technical font. The ink is significantly raised (3D puff/high-density effect). Cinematic lighting, sharp focus on the ink texture, architectural fitness aesthetic, 8k.`

## Design-056: "The Y2K Layer-Block"
*   **Name:** The Y2K Layer-Block
*   **Design Intent/Rationale:** Inspired by the 2026 trend for "layered Y2K tops" and "vibrant color blocking." This design creates a visual illusion of a layered t-shirt (co-ord style) by using a massive block of ink across the bottom half, contrasting with a primary color base.
*   **Technical Breakdown:**
    *   **Placement:** Bottom 40% Body Wrap (Full circle)
    *   **Ink:** Matte Forest Green (Base Block) + High-Gloss Black Typography
    *   **Garment:** Optic White 240 GSM
*   **Gemini Studio Prompt:**
    `A high-resolution product shot of an oversized optic white t-shirt. The bottom 40% of the garment is completely covered in a solid, matte Forest Green screen print, creating a 'layered' visual effect. Overlapping this green block is the word 'ALPHANIX' in massive, high-gloss black typography that spans the entire width and bleeds off the side seams. The contrast between the matte green and the shiny black ink is highly visible. Clean studio background, minimal Y2K streetwear vibe, 8k.`
