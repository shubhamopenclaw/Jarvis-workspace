# Design Concept: ALPHANIX 005 - 040326-19-00

## CONCEPT 1: THE LOUD ONE - "T-DOT WIREFRAME ARCHITECT"
**Aesthetic:** Abstract Geometry / Institutional Athleticism
**Placement:** Large Back (Shoulder to Shoulder) + Minimal Front Left Chest

### Technical Breakdown
- **Placement:** Front: 4" wide Left Chest. Back: 14" x 16" Center.
- **Max Dimensions:** Back: 14" x 16".
- **Ink:** High-density White Plastisol (Puff effect on the wireframe lines).
- **Design Details:** A complex, eroded wireframe silhouette of the CN Tower and Toronto skyline, integrated with "SECTOR 6IX" in vertical Bebas Neue typography. The lines are broken and staggered, giving it a technical blueprint feel rather than a skyline photo.

### Prompt 1: The "Studio" Shot (Verification)
A hyper-realistic professional studio photograph of the ALPHANIX Primary Model Identity wearing a premium heavyweight oversized cotton t-shirt in Black colour. The model stands confidently, back turned slightly to show the large graphic, against a soft off-white seamless studio background with subtle gradient shadow. High-end commercial fitness lighting with soft overhead key light and controlled rim light outlining the shoulders and traps. The graphic on the back is an eroded technical wireframe of a skyline integrated with vertical bold typography "SECTOR 6IX", printed in thick white colour using matte screen-print ink with a high-density puff texture. Allow only natural fabric folds. Shot on an 85mm lens, photorealistic, sharp focus, clean editorial campaign aesthetic, rugged luxury fitness brand.

### Prompt 2: The "Atmospheric" Shot (Vibe)
A cinematic editorial photograph of a premium heavyweight oversized t-shirt in Black colour hanging naturally from a matte black wooden hanger on a tree branch outdoors. The back graphic, featuring a technical wireframe skyline and "SECTOR 6IX" vertical text, is printed with a matte white plastisol screen-printing ink. The Toronto skyline is visible in the background at sunrise, softly out of focus with shallow depth of field (85mm lens look). Early morning warm natural light casts subtle highlights and realistic shadows on the fabric. Slight breeze movement creates natural cotton drape. Photorealistic, high-end fashion campaign aesthetic, minimal color grading, rugged luxury mood.

## CONCEPT 2: THE QUIET ONE - "INSTITUTIONAL MONOLITH"
**Aesthetic:** Quiet Luxury / Typography & Composition
**Placement:** Center Chest (Small/Micro)

### Technical Breakdown
- **Placement:** Center Chest, 2 inches below collar.
- **Max Dimensions:** 6" wide x 1.5" high.
- **Ink:** Flat White Plastisol.
- **Design Details:** The word "ALPHANIX" in widely spaced geometric sans-serif (Montserrat Light), with the coordinates of Toronto (43.6532° N, 79.3832° W) printed in tiny 8pt font immediately below it, right-aligned to the last 'X'. Minimalist, architectural, and disciplined.

### Prompt 1: The "Studio" Shot (Verification)
A hyper-realistic professional studio photograph of the ALPHANIX Primary Model Identity wearing a premium heavyweight oversized cotton t-shirt in Black colour. The model stands confidently facing forward against a soft off-white seamless studio background with subtle gradient shadow. High-end commercial fitness lighting with soft overhead key light and controlled rim light outlining the shoulders and traps. The graphic on the center chest is minimalist "ALPHANIX" text with small coordinates below it, printed in clean matte white colour using premium screen-print ink. Allow only natural fabric folds. Shot on an 85mm lens, photorealistic, sharp focus, clean editorial campaign aesthetic, rugged luxury fitness brand.

### Prompt 2: The "Atmospheric" Shot (Vibe)
A cinematic editorial photograph of a premium heavyweight oversized t-shirt in Black colour hanging naturally from a matte black wooden hanger on a tree branch outdoors. The Toronto skyline is visible in the background at sunrise, softly out of focus with shallow depth of field (85mm lens look). Early morning warm natural light casts subtle highlights and realistic shadows on the fabric. Slight breeze movement creates natural cotton drape. Photorealistic, high-end fashion campaign aesthetic, minimal color grading, rugged luxury mood.
