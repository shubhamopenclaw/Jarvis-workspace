# ALPHANIX Design-021 [060326-03-00]

## Concept 1: LOUD ("REDACTED IDENTITY")
**Theme:** Redacted Identity / Technical Erasure
**Description:** A massive horizontal "ALPHANIX" on the high back where the top half of the typography is "erased" or redacted, creating a sharp horizontal split. Below it, a vertical list of Toronto area codes (416, 647, 437) in a sharp technical stencil font, each struck through with a single horizontal line.

### Technical Breakdown
- **Placement:** High Back (Nape to Mid-back)
- **Max Dimensions:** 14" x 8"
- **Ink:** White Plastisol (High Opacity)
- **Typography:** Heavy Bold Sans-Serif (ALPHANIX), Technical Stencil (Codes).

### Gemini Prompts (Optimized for nano-banana-pro)

**Prompt 1: The "Studio" Shot (Verification)**
> A hyper-realistic professional studio photograph of the ALPHANIX Primary Model Identity wearing a premium heavyweight oversized cotton t-shirt in Black colour. The model stands with back to the camera, looking slightly over the left shoulder. On the high back, a massive 14-inch wide "ALPHANIX" graphic is screen-printed in thick white ink; the top half of the letters is clean-cut/erased as if redacted. Below the brand name, a vertical column of Toronto area codes "416", "647", "437" in a sharp technical stencil font, each struck through with a single thin horizontal line. High-end commercial fitness lighting, photorealistic, sharp focus on the back graphic, 85mm lens.

**Prompt 2: The "Atmospheric" Shot (Vibe)**
> A cinematic editorial photograph of a premium heavyweight oversized t-shirt in Black colour hanging naturally from a matte black wooden hanger on a industrial metal fence outdoors. The "REDACTED IDENTITY" graphic (massive white ALPHANIX text with top-half erasure and struck-through area codes) is printed with matte white plastisol ink. The Toronto skyline is visible in the background at dawn, softly out of focus. Early morning cool light casts subtle highlights on the fabric texture. Photorealistic, high-end fashion campaign aesthetic, rugged luxury mood, 85mm lens look.

## Concept 2: QUIET ("FOUNDATION DATA")
**Theme:** Foundation Data / Hem Technical
**Description:** Extreme minimalist placement at the bottom left hem. A small horizontal block featuring wide-kerned branding, a boxed unit number, and technical fabric classification.

### Technical Breakdown
- **Placement:** Bottom Left Hem (Front)
- **Max Dimensions:** 4" x 2"
- **Ink:** White Plastisol
- **Typography:** Montserrat Light (Wide Kerned), Monospace (Protocol).

### Gemini Prompts (Optimized for nano-banana-pro)

**Prompt 1: The "Studio" Shot (Verification)**
> A hyper-realistic professional studio photograph of the ALPHANIX Primary Model Identity wearing a premium heavyweight oversized cotton t-shirt in Black colour. The camera angle is low, focusing on the bottom left hem of the shirt. A small, minimalist horizontal text block is printed in matte white: "ALPHANIX" in ultra-light wide-kerned font, followed by a small "01" inside a thin square box. Directly below, "HEAVYWEIGHT PROTOCOL" is printed in a micro-monospace font. High-end commercial lighting, extreme sharp focus on the fabric weave and ink texture, 85mm macro lens.

**Prompt 2: The "Atmospheric" Shot (Vibe)**
> A cinematic editorial photograph of a premium heavyweight oversized t-shirt in Black colour. The shot is a close-up on the bottom hem as it rests on a raw concrete surface in an urban Toronto setting. The "FOUNDATION DATA" technical branding (minimalist white text and boxed "01") is crisp against the black cotton fabric. Soft morning light creates long shadows across the concrete. Photorealistic, minimal color grading, high-end fashion detail shot, 85mm lens.
