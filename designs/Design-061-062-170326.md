# Design-061: "The Monolith Oblique"
*   **Name:** The Monolith Oblique
*   **Date:** 17-03-26
*   **Design Intent/Rationale:** Targets the 2026 "Retro-Athletic" and "Bold Statement" trends. Moves away from centered or symmetric placements to an aggressive, oversized "Oblique Cut" that breaks the side seam. The use of a "Cracked Earth" texture in the ink gives it a rugged, vintage streetwear edge while maintaining the ALPHANIX "Institutional" discipline.
*   **Technical Breakdown:**
    *   **Placement:** Massive Diagonal Side-Wrap (Left Shoulder down to Right Hip)
    *   **Ink:** Off-White "Heavy Crackle" Plastisol (intentionally weathered)
    *   **Garment:** Deep Navy Blue 300 GSM Heavyweight Cotton
*   **Gemini Studio Prompt:**
    `A hyper-realistic professional studio photograph of a premium 300 GSM Deep Navy Blue heavyweight t-shirt. A massive "ALPHANIX" wordmark is printed in an oversized oblique angle, running from the left shoulder down across the chest to the right bottom hip. The ink is a thick Off-White with a heavy "cracked earth" texture, giving it a vintage, weathered athletic look. The text bleeds off the side seam and the shoulder. Cinematic lighting, sharp focus on the ink's textural decay, elite retro-fitness streetwear aesthetic, 8k.`

# Design-062: "The Chromatic Pulse-Line"
*   **Name:** The Chromatic Pulse-Line
*   **Date:** 17-03-26
*   **Design Intent/Rationale:** Embraces the 2026 trend of "Neon Colors" and "Digital Digital Prints" combined with "Anatomical Mapping." Instead of a full graphic, it uses a single, continuous ultra-thin "Pulse-Line" that maps the V-taper of the back, creating a technical, high-performance visual.
*   **Technical Breakdown:**
    *   **Placement:** Full Back (V-Taper Mapping - Nape to Lats)
    *   **Ink:** Neon Citron-to-Electric Cyan Gradient (Split Fountain) + Clear 3D High-Gloss Gel Overlay
    *   **Garment:** Matte Black 240 GSM
*   **Gemini Studio Prompt:**
    `A high-resolution product shot from the rear of a premium matte black 240 GSM t-shirt. A single, continuous ultra-thin "Pulse-Line" graphic in a Neon Citron to Electric Cyan gradient maps the natural V-taper of the back muscles, starting at the nape and curving out toward the lats. The word "ALPHANIX" is micro-printed along the line near the neck in a technical font. The line has a high-gloss 3D gel finish that catches the light like a fiber-optic cable. Deep shadows, futuristic technical fitness vibe, 8k.`
