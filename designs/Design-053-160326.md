# ALPHANIX Design Concept: Design-053-160326
**Theme: Prismatic Distortion & Radial Geometry**

## Design-053: "The Radial Halo"
*   **Name:** The Radial Halo
*   **Design Intent/Rationale:** Moving away from the recent "Liquid" and "Brutalist Block" themes, this design explores **Radial Symmetry**. It leans into the 2026 trend of "Digital Prints" and "Primary Colors" but applies it in a strictly geometric, halo-like placement around the neck. This frames the face and emphasizes the shoulder width without using standard chest blocks.
*   **Technical Breakdown:**
    *   **Placement:** Radial Neck/Shoulder (360-degree 'Halo' around the collar)
    *   **Ink:** Primary Cyan + Matte White (Overlapping transparent layers)
    *   **Garment:** Optic White 300 GSM Heavyweight
*   **Gemini Studio Prompt:**
    `A hyper-realistic professional studio photograph of a premium 300 GSM optic white heavyweight t-shirt. The shirt features a 'Radial Halo' graphic consisting of concentric geometric rings and technical markings encircling the entire collar and upper shoulder area. The rings are printed in overlapping layers of Primary Cyan and Matte White ink, creating a subtle third-tone where they intersect. The word 'ALPHANIX' is repeated in a micro-typographic font along one of the outer rings. Cinematic lighting, sharp focus on the collar detail, high-end technical streetwear aesthetic, 8k.`

## Design-054: "The Kinetic Fracture"
*   **Name:** The Kinetic Fracture
*   **Design Intent/Rationale:** A sharp departure from previous "Fluid" or "Architectural" designs. This uses **Prismatic Fracture** but applied to the **Sleeve-to-Rib** connection point. It captures the 2026 "neon/bold color" trend using a high-visibility Electric Lime, focusing on the "Active Motion" of the arm. It treats the garment as a kinetic sculpture.
*   **Technical Breakdown:**
    *   **Placement:** Right Sleeve to Right Rib (Discontinuous print that aligns when the arm is down)
    *   **Ink:** High-Density Electric Lime (Neon)
    *   **Garment:** Matte Black 300 GSM
*   **Gemini Studio Prompt:**
    `A professional product shot of a premium matte black oversized t-shirt. A sharp, geometric 'fracture' graphic in High-Density Electric Lime neon ink starts on the right sleeve and continues onto the side rib area. The graphic is designed to look like a single continuous line that has been shattered into shards. The 'ALPHANIX' wordmark is integrated into the shards on the rib section. The ink is raised and tactile with a slight neon glow. Shot from a side profile, dramatic shadows, futuristic athletic vibe, 8k.`
