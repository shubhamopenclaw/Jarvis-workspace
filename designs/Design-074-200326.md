# ALPHANIX Design Concept: Design-074
**Date:** 20-03-2026
**Name:** The Solar-Shift Heat-Gusset

## Design Intent/Rationale
This design captures the 2026 "Bold Aesthetics" and "Vibrant Digital Prints" by focusing on the body's natural heat zones—the underarms and side gussets. Instead of a traditional centered graphic, "The Solar-Shift" uses a massive, solid color-block that "bleeds" from a Deep Purple into a Neon Orange, simulating a thermal heat-map. The inclusion of a warped "Liquid Chrome" silver wordmark across the lower back hem adds a premium, futuristic "molten" element to the piece.

## Technical Breakdown
*   **Garment:** Void Black 240 GSM Luxury Oversized Fit.
*   **Placement:** 
    *   **Side-Gussets:** A massive vertical block that starts at the underarm and tapers down to the mid-rib.
    *   **Inner-Sleeves:** The print continues from the underarm into the inner biceps of the sleeves.
*   **Ink/Texture:** Solar-Shift Gradient (Neon Orange to Deep Purple) + High-Gloss "Liquid Chrome" Silver.
*   **Branding:** The 'ALPHANIX' wordmark is printed in "Liquid Chrome" across the lower back hem, but the letters are "warped" and stretched horizontally, as if distorted by heat.
