# [31-03-26] Drop: Neo-Grunge Utility & Retro-Futurist Fluidity

## Design-145: THE NEO-GRUNGE UTILITY-BLOCK
- **Vibe**: 2026 Neo-Grunge / Industrial Utility. A transition from clean technical blocks to raw, distressed performance-wear. High-impact, heavy, and weathered.
- **Placement**: Massive Asymmetrical 270-degree "Utility-Sling" (Right Shoulder-Cap -> Center Chest -> Left Hem).
- **Ink**: Industrial Charcoal (Heavy-Crackle Base) + Neon Safety Orange (High-Gloss "Warning" Overlayer) + Phantom Black (3D Micro-Stitch Detailing).
- **Gemini Prompt (Studio Shot)**: Front-diagonal dynamic studio photograph of a 'Void Black' 300 GSM heavyweight luxury t-shirt. Massive, aggressive 'NEO-GRUNGE UTILITY-BLOCK' graphic in distressed 'Industrial Charcoal' and high-gloss 'Neon Safety Orange' sweeping from the right shoulder to the left hem. The 'ALPHANIX' wordmark is vertically integrated into the charcoal block in high-gloss black. 8k.

## Design-146: THE RETRO-FUTURIST FLUID-WRAP
- **Vibe**: 2026 "Gym-to-Street" Fluidity / 90s Retro-Futurism. Combining the organic, liquid aesthetics of 2026 with the vibrant, high-contrast color palettes of the late 90s.
- **Placement**: Massive 360-degree Lower-Torso "Fluid-Orbit" (Mid-Ab to Lower-Hem) + Right Sleeve "Pulse-Ring".
- **Ink**: Electric Cobalt to Solar Yellow (Split-Fountain) + 3D Clear "Liquid Silicone" (Surface Tension Ridges).
- **Gemini Prompt (Studio Shot)**: Low-angle dynamic studio photograph of an 'Optic White' 240 GSM luxury oversized t-shirt. A massive, organic 'RETRO-FUTURIST FLUID-WRAP' graphic in a vibrant 'Electric Cobalt' to 'Solar Yellow' gradient wrapping the lower torso 360-degrees. Raised 3D clear 'Liquid Silicone' ridges map the surface tension of the fluid. 'ALPHANIX' is distorted into the liquid flow on the front-left hip. 8k.
