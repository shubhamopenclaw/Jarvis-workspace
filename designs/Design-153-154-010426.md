**Concept**: Tapping into the 2026 trend of 'Iridescent Neutrals' and tech-infused color palettes. This design features a central atmospheric 'pulse' that shifts colors based on light, grounded in a minimalist structure.

> "A professional studio shot of a 240 GSM Dark Gray oversized t-shirt. ## Design 154: THE SCULPTURAL KINETIC-VANE
**Concept**: Inspired by the 'contrast piping' and 'seam detailing' trend. This design uses bold, directional graphics to act as 'vanes' that guide the eye along the body's kinetic lines.
**Gemini Prompt**: 
> "A studio shot of a 200 GSM Black regular fit t-shirt. From the left shoulder down to the right hip, a series of thick, sculptural 'vane' graphics are printed in high-density 'Electric Cobalt' ink. These vanes have a 3D, ribbed texture that follows the anatomical contour of the torso. The word 'ALPHANIX' is vertically integrated into the last vane on the lower right hip in a clean, bold Montserrat font. Professional product photography, sharp focus on the 3D ribbed ink, clean background, 8k."
