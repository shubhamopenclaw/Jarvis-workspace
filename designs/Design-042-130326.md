# ALPHANIX Design Concept: Design-042-130326
**Theme:** Y2K Layered / Digital Foliage

## Design-042: "The Overgrown Logic"
*   **Name:** The Overgrown Logic
*   **Design Intent/Rationale:** Inspired by the 2026 trend of "Digital Prints" and "Layered Y2K Aesthetics." This design features an "Electric Forest Green" colorway (trending for 2026 kicks/apparel). The placement is a "Bottom-Up Rise"—the graphic starts heavy at the hem and fades out as it moves up the torso, like digital vines or data-corruption 'overgrowth.' It moves away from the 'clean blueprint' into something more organic and aggressive.
*   **Technical Breakdown:**
    *   **Placement:** Lower Half Wrap (Rising from Hem)
    *   **Ink:** Matte Sage Green + Metallic Emerald Dual-Tone Plastisol
    *   **Garment:** Electric Forest Green 240 GSM Oversized
*   **Gemini Studio Prompt:**
    `A hyper-realistic professional studio photograph of the ALPHANIX Primary Model Identity wearing a premium oversized t-shirt in Electric Forest Green. A complex, dual-tone graphic in Matte Sage and Metallic Emerald Green rises from the bottom hem, wrapping around the entire lower torso. The graphic is a 'digital overgrowth' pattern—half-organic vine, half-circuitry. The ALPHANIX name is fragmented and hidden within the pattern near the left hip. 50mm lens, dynamic lighting to highlight the metallic ink contrast, high-energy futuristic streetwear aesthetic.`
