# ALPHANIX Design Concept: 20-03-26 (PM)
Theme: **High-Contrast Kineticism & Brutalist Utility**

## Design-075: "The Neon Rib-Vent"
*   **Name:** The Neon Rib-Vent
*   **Design Intent/Rationale:** Targets the 2026 "Bold Neon" and "Minimalist" trends. Instead of a standard front print, this focuses on the **Side-Rib and Lateral Serratus** area. It uses a high-impact Neon Electric Yellow to frame the athlete's physique, creating a "ventilation" illusion through graphic blocks. It aligns with the "Baggy Rebellion" by using an oversized fit but adding structural "weight" to the sides.
*   **Technical Breakdown:**
    *   **Placement:** Vertical Side-Rib (Left side only) + Inner-Bicep (Right side only).
    *   **Ink:** Neon Electric Yellow (Matte) + High-Density "Void Black" (3D Border).
    *   **Garment:** Deep Cobalt Blue 240 GSM Luxury Oversized.
*   **Gemini Studio Prompt:**
    `A side-profile professional studio photograph of a Deep Cobalt Blue 240 GSM luxury oversized t-shirt. A massive vertical 'Neon Rib-Vent' graphic in Neon Electric Yellow runs from the left armpit down to the hem. The graphic consists of thick horizontal bars of yellow ink, each with a raised 3D high-density 'Void Black' border. The 'ALPHANIX' wordmark is vertically aligned in small, crisp black technical typography within the bottom yellow bar. Sharp studio lighting, focus on the vibrant neon and the 3D texture against the deep blue fabric. 8k.`

## Design-076: "The Monolith Nape-Wrap"
*   **Name:** The Monolith Nape-Wrap
*   **Design Intent/Rationale:** Inspired by the 2026 "Contrast Piping" and "Industrial" aesthetics. It shifts the entire visual focus to the **Upper Back and Shoulder-Cap**, wrapping forward slightly onto the traps. It uses a "Concrete Grey" base with a "Primary Red" block, creating a brutalist, high-performance look that emphasizes the upper-body frame.
*   **Technical Breakdown:**
    *   **Placement:** Upper Back (Shoulder-to-Shoulder) + Front Traps "Neck-Harness" Wrap.
    *   **Ink:** Primary Red (Solid Matte Block) + "Liquid Chrome" Silver (Micro-Typography).
    *   **Garment:** Concrete Grey 300 GSM Heavyweight.
*   **Gemini Studio Prompt:**
    `A high-angle rear-diagonal product shot of a Concrete Grey 300 GSM heavyweight t-shirt. A solid, massive 'Monolith' block in matte Primary Red covers the entire upper back from shoulder-to-shoulder and wraps over the top of the traps onto the front collarbone area. In the center of the red block at the nape of the neck, the 'ALPHANIX' wordmark is printed in ultra-reflective 'Liquid Chrome' silver. The silver ink has a metallic, mirror-like finish. Brutalist industrial fitness aesthetic, high-contrast editorial lighting. 8k.`
