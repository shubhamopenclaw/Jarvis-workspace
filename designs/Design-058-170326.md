# ALPHANIX Design Concept: THE DIGITAL GRADIENT OVERLAY
**ID:** Design-058-170326
**Theme:** Digital Fluidity & Vibrant Contrast

## Design Intent / Rationale
Capturing the 2026 "Vibrant Digital Prints" and "Bold Color" trends. This design moves away from architectural lines into a "Digital Wash" aesthetic. It uses an unconventional "Side-Body Rise" placement—starting from the bottom-right hem and washing up toward the center-chest. The graphic is an abstract, vibrant digital texture that feels like a glitching lava lamp.

## Strike Zone Check
- [x] No technical blocks
- [x] No 'Sector 6ix' / 'Ops T-Dot'
- [x] Brand name 'ALPHANIX' only
- [x] Unique placement (Side-Body Hem Rise)
- [x] 2026 Trend: Digital Prints / Neon-Primary Mixing

## Technical Breakdown
- **Placement:** Bottom-Right Hem to Center-Chest (Diagonal Wash)
- **Ink:** Multi-tone Split Fountain (Neon Emerald to Primary Green)
- **Garment:** Optic White 240 GSM (Oversized Drop-Shoulder)
- **Typography:** ALPHANIX wordmark is distorted and submerged within the digital green wash, appearing in negative space (white fabric).
