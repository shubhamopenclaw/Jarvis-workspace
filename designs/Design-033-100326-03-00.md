# ALPHANIX Design: [100326-03-00] Design-033

## 🧬 Concept 1: LOUD ("INDUSTRIAL KINETICS")
**Aesthetic:** Heavy typographic overlap with architectural wireframes. High-energy, bold, and technically dense.

### 📐 Technical Breakdown
- **Placement:** Full Back (High)
- **Max Dimensions:** 14" x 16"
- **Ink:** White Plastisol (Thick, High Opacity)
- **Typography:** Bebas Neue (Condensed Bold) for main text; Montserrat (Light) for technical data.

### 🖼️ Prompt 1: The "Studio" Shot (Verification)
A hyper-realistic professional studio photograph of the ALPHANIX Primary Model Identity wearing a premium heavyweight oversized cotton t-shirt in Black colour. The model stands with back to camera, looking slightly over the shoulder. High-end commercial fitness lighting with soft overhead key light and controlled rim light outlining the shoulders and traps. The graphic features a massive, vertically stacked "ALPHANIX" in bold white Bebas Neue, intersected by a horizontal "glitch" bar containing micro-text "PROTOCOL-033 // SECTOR-6IX". A thin, architectural isometric wireframe of a city block is layered behind the text, creating depth. Printed in thick white colour using matte screen-print ink. Allow only natural fabric folds. Shot on an 85mm lens, photorealistic, sharp focus, clean editorial campaign aesthetic, rugged luxury fitness brand.

### 🖼️ Prompt 2: The "Atmospheric" Shot (Vibe)
A cinematic editorial photograph of a premium heavyweight oversized t-shirt in Black colour hanging naturally from a matte black wooden hanger on a tree branch outdoors. The graphic on the back features massive, vertically stacked "ALPHANIX" in white, intersected by a horizontal "glitch" bar and a subtle isometric wireframe city block. Printed with a matte white plastisol screen-printing ink. The Toronto skyline is visible in the background at sunrise, softly out of focus with shallow depth of field (85mm lens look). Early morning warm natural light casts subtle highlights and realistic shadows on the fabric. Slight breeze movement creates natural cotton drape. Photorealistic, high-end fashion campaign aesthetic, minimal color grading, rugged luxury mood.

## 🧬 Concept 2: QUIET ("MONOLITH SUBSET")
**Aesthetic:** Extreme precision, asymmetric balance, and micro-typographic technicality. "Quiet Luxury" for the disciplined.

### 📐 Technical Breakdown
- **Placement:** Left Ribcage (Vertical) + Nape of Neck (Center)
- **Max Dimensions:** 2" x 10" (Rib), 3" x 1" (Neck)
- **Ink:** Matte Grey Plastisol (Low Contrast)
- **Typography:** Montserrat (Wide Kerning)

### 🖼️ Prompt 1: The "Studio" Shot (Verification)
A hyper-realistic professional studio photograph of the ALPHANIX Primary Model Identity wearing a premium heavyweight oversized cotton t-shirt in Black colour. The model stands in a side profile, showcasing the left ribcage. High-end commercial fitness lighting with soft overhead key light and controlled rim light. The graphic is a minimalist vertical line of text running down the left ribcage: "ALPHANIX / UNIT-033 / TORONTO" in small, ultra-wide kerned geometric sans-serif. On the nape of the neck, a tiny outlined box contains the number "01". Printed in subtle matte grey colour using premium screen-print ink. Allow only natural fabric folds. Shot on an 85mm lens, photorealistic, sharp focus, clean editorial campaign aesthetic, rugged luxury fitness brand.

The graphic on the left ribcage is a minimalist vertical line "ALPHANIX / UNIT-033 / TORONTO" in tiny wide-kerned white text, with a small box "01" on the neck. Printed with a matte grey plastisol screen-printing ink. The Toronto skyline is visible in the background at sunrise, softly out of focus with shallow depth of field (85mm lens look). Early morning warm natural light casts subtle highlights and realistic shadows on the fabric. Slight breeze movement creates natural cotton drape. Photorealistic, high-end fashion campaign aesthetic, minimal color grading, rugged luxury mood.
