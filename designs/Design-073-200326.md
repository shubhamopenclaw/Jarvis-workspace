# ALPHANIX Design Concept: Design-073
**Date:** 20-03-2026
**Name:** The Primary Pulse-Stitch

## Design Intent/Rationale
This design leverages the 2026 "Primary Color" and "Contrast Piping" trends by introducing a high-impact, structural element that emphasizes the garment's architecture. Moving away from the typical shoulder-wrap, "The Pulse-Stitch" focuses on the verticality of the spine—symbolizing the athlete's core strength—and the circularity of the neck. The use of a "Sandstone" base color provides a sophisticated, industrial contrast to the vibrant Primary Red ink, differentiating it from previous dark-palette releases.

## Technical Breakdown
*   **Garment:** Sandstone (Industrial Tan) 300 GSM Heavyweight Boxy Fit.
*   **Placement:** 
    *   **Front:** A single, ultra-fine 3D silicone line circles the base of the collar.
    *   **Back:** The line breaks at the nape and drops perfectly straight down the center spine to the bottom hem.
*   **Ink/Texture:** Primary Red 3D High-Density Silicone (2mm height).
*   **Branding:** The 'ALPHANIX' wordmark is micro-printed in white technical font *inside* the red silicone line, repeating every 5cm like a DNA sequence.
