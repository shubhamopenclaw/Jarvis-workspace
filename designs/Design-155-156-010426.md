# ALPHANIX Design Concepts - 01/04/26

## Design-155: THE OBLIQUE MONOLITH
**Aesthetic:** Brutalist Utility / Vertical Asymmetry
**Graphic Placement:** A single, massive vertical "ALPHANIX" wordmark running from the bottom hem up to the mid-chest, strictly on the left-side seam.
**Concept Detail:** 
- Instead of centered branding, this uses extreme negative space.
- The typography is a custom, high-density "Concrete Gray" puff print on a "Carbon Black" oversized blank.
- The font is a heavy, blocky sans-serif where the 'A' and 'X' are slightly elongated to emphasize the verticality.
- No other graphics. The impact comes from the sheer scale and the unconventional "off-axis" placement that breaks the traditional T-shirt focal point.

## Design-156: THE KINETIC SHADOW-WRAP
**Aesthetic:** Stealth Performance / Motion-Blur
**Graphic Placement:** A wrap-around graphic that starts at the right kidney/lower back and curves upward across the ribs to the front-left shoulder.
**Concept Detail:**
- The word 'ALPHANIX' is distorted as if captured in a high-speed motion blur. 
- It isn't a solid print; it’s a "shatter-gradient" using a mix of Matte Black and Gloss Black inks (Black-on-Black).
- The text is only legible from specific angles, appearing as a sleek, organic "rib" structure from afar.
- This design emphasizes the athlete's physique and movement, following the natural torque of the body during a lift or sprint.
- Strictly brand name only, integrated into the motion-blur pattern.
