# Design Concept: THE NEON-GRAFFITI CHASSIS
**Design-137-290326**

## Visual Vibe
**2026 Urban-Athletic Fusion / Expressive Technicality.**
This design merges the raw, hand-drawn energy of 2026 graffiti trends with the structured, anatomical "chassis" mapping of elite fitness streetwear. It moves away from rigid geometric blocks into expressive, high-energy typography and fluid linework.

## Placement
- **Primary**: Massive Asymmetrical Wrap. Starts from the **Left Trapezius/Shoulder**, sweeps diagonally across the **Center Chest (Sternum)**, and terminates in a "shattered" effect on the **Right Lower Rib/Hem**.
- **Secondary**: A single "Hand-Drawn" ALPHANIX tag on the **Back-Right Scapula**.

## Ink & Texture
- **Base Layer**: 'Electric Cobalt' Matte (High-Opacity).
- **Overlayer**: 'Neon Toxic Green' Hand-Drawn "Scribble" lines.
- **Topcoat**: 3D High-Gloss 'Crystal' Gel on the 'ALPHANIX' typography within the scribble, creating a "wet-paint" effect.

## Gemini Prompt (Studio Shot)
> Front-diagonal dynamic studio photograph of a 'Void Black' 240 GSM luxury oversized t-shirt. The 'NEON-GRAFFITI CHASSIS' graphic features a massive, aggressive sweep of hand-drawn graffiti-style 'ALPHANIX' typography in 'Electric Cobalt' and 'Neon Toxic Green'. The graphic starts at the left shoulder and cuts diagonally across the chest to the right hem. The ink has a visible 'wet-paint' high-gloss finish on the green accents, contrasting with the matte black fabric. 8k, hyper-modern urban fitness aesthetic.

# Design Concept: THE KINETIC PIPING-HALO
**Design-138-290326**

## Visual Vibe
**2026 High-Tech Performance / Sculptural Minimalism.**
Focusing on the "contrast piping and seam detailing" trend identified for 2026. This is a "quiet luxury" technical piece that uses 3D structural elements to "sculpt" the athlete's physique without traditional large-scale graphics.

## Placement
- **Collar**: 360-degree "Double-Pipe" wrap around the neckline.
- **Sleeves**: Vertical "Pulse-Lines" running from the **Shoulder-Cap down to the Cuff** on both arms.
- **Back**: Symmetrical "Scapular-Arch" piping that follows the natural curve of the shoulder blades.

## Ink & Texture
- **Material**: 3mm High-Density 'Industrial Charcoal' Silicone (Matte).
- **Accents**: 'Primary Red' micro-dots at the "connection points" (where the piping meets the collar and cuffs).
- **Branding**: 'ALPHANIX' is vertically embossed *into* the silicone piping on the right sleeve.

## Gemini Prompt (Studio Shot)
> High-angle rear-view minimalist studio photograph of a 'Stone Grey' 300 GSM heavyweight boxy t-shirt. The design features raised 3mm 'Industrial Charcoal' matte silicone piping mapping the collar and the natural curves of the scapula (shoulder blades). Fine 'Primary Red' micro-dots are placed at the structural intersections of the piping. The 'ALPHANIX' brand name is subtly embossed into the vertical silicone line on the right sleeve. 8k, elite technical fitness vibe.
