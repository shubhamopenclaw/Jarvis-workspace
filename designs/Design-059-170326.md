# ALPHANIX Design Concept: Design-059-170326
Theme: **Graffiti-Tech & Expressive Typography (2026 Trend)**

## Design-059: "The Scribble Core"
*   **Name:** The Scribble Core
*   **Design Intent/Rationale:** Directly leans into the 2026 "Graffiti-inspired graphics and hand-drawn illustrations" trend. It breaks away from the brand's usual rigid, architectural geometry into a "controlled chaos" aesthetic. The goal is to make the brand feel more raw and human while maintaining the high-end fitness streetwear edge.
*   **Technical Breakdown:**
    *   **Placement:** Massive Wrap-Around (Right Shoulder to Left Hip Diagonal)
    *   **Ink:** Matte Neon Citron (Hand-drawn "Scribble") + High-Gloss Black (Stencil 'ALPHANIX')
    *   **Garment:** Heavyweight Off-White (Bone) 300 GSM
*   **Gemini Studio Prompt:**
    `A hyper-realistic professional studio photograph of a premium 300 GSM heavyweight t-shirt in Off-White (Bone color). The shirt features a massive, expressive "scribble" graphic in Matte Neon Citron ink that sweeps diagonally from the right shoulder down to the left hip. Superimposed over the chaotic citron lines is the word "ALPHANIX" in a sharp, high-gloss black stencil font. The contrast between the hand-drawn neon texture and the precise glossy black typography is the focal point. Cinematic lighting, sharp focus on the ink layers, raw urban fitness aesthetic, 8k.`

## Design-060: "The Neon Piping Illusion"
*   **Name:** The Neon Piping Illusion
*   **Design Intent/Rationale:** Combines "Wearable Tech" aesthetics with "Contrast Piping" (2026 trends). It uses high-density printing to create the illusion of glowing fiber-optic cables integrated into the garment's structure. This is a "clean" counterpart to Design-059.
*   **Technical Breakdown:**
    *   **Placement:** Side-Seam and Underarm "Vents" (Anatomical)
    *   **Ink:** High-Density "Electric Violet" Silicone (Glow Effect)
    *   **Garment:** Matte Black 300 GSM
*   **Gemini Studio Prompt:**
    `A high-resolution product shot of a premium 300 GSM matte black heavyweight t-shirt. The shirt features thin, high-density lines of "Electric Violet" silicone ink running vertically along the side seams and curving into the underarm area, simulating glowing fiber-optic piping. The word "ALPHANIX" is printed in a micro-technical font in the same violet ink on the lower right rib. The ink has a rounded 3D profile that catches the light like a physical cable. Moody studio lighting, futuristic tech-wear vibe, 8k.`
