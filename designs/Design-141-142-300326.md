# Design-141: THE RADIAL KINETIC-CORE
- **Date**: 30-03-26
- **Vibe**: 2026 "Gym-to-Street" Energy / Centripetal Power. A move away from linear blocks toward circular, high-energy focal points.
- **Placement**: Massive Asymmetrical Radial Burst (Right Pec to Right Lat/Ribs) + Right Sleeve "Orbit-Ring".
- **Ink**: Neon Solar-Orange (Solid Matte) + Primary Red (Halftone "Motion-Blur") + 3D Clear "Liquid Silicone" (Center-Point).
- **Description**: A powerful, circular graphic that mimics a centrifugal force, exploding from the right pectoral and wrapping around the side-ribs. The core features a dense ALPHANIX wordmark warped into a spiral, while the outer rings are composed of vibrant Neon Solar-Orange and Primary Red halftone gradients that simulate high-speed rotation.

A massive 'RADIAL KINETIC-CORE' graphic in vibrant 'Neon Solar-Orange' and 'Primary Red' halftones exploding from the right chest and wrapping the ribs. High-gloss 'Liquid Silicone' center-point. 8k.

# Design-142: THE SCULPTURAL TRAP-MANTLE
- **Date**: 30-03-26
- **Vibe**: 2026 Sculptural Minimalism / Anatomical Architecture. Framing the traps and neck to emphasize athletic posture.
- **Placement**: 360-degree Collar "Mantle" (3-inch width) + Symmetrical Trapezius "Wings".
- **Ink**: Industrial Charcoal (3mm High-Density Matte Silicone) + 'Phantom Grey' (Matte Tonal Micro-Dots).
- **Description**: A heavy, architectural mantle that frames the neck and shoulders. The 3mm high-density silicone creates a literal "shell" effect over the trapezius muscles. The ALPHANIX wordmark is embossed into the charcoal silicone on the nape of the neck, visible only through texture and shadow (Tonal-Luxury).
- **Gemini Prompt (Studio Shot)**: High-angle rear-view minimalist studio photograph of a 'Bone' Off-White 300 GSM heavyweight boxy t-shirt. Raised 3mm 'Industrial Charcoal' matte silicone 'mantle' mapping the collar and trapezius muscles. 'ALPHANIX' embossed into the silicone at the nape. 8k.
