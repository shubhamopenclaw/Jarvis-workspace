# ALPHANIX Design Concepts: 2026-03-23 (Extra-Drop)
Theme: **Primary Primary & Negative Sculpting**

## Design-087: "The Primary X-Lock"
*   **Name:** The Primary X-Lock
*   **Design Intent/Rationale:** Directly responds to the 2026 "Primary Colors" and "Contrast Seam" trends. Instead of traditional side-stripes, this design creates a massive, centered "X" using two intersecting bars of Primary Red and Primary Blue. The intersection occurs at the center-chest (Sternum), framing the athlete's pectorals and obliques. It avoids the "technical block" look by using a "brushed-stroke" digital texture within the bars, making them look like raw paint rather than a digital asset.
*   **Technical Breakdown:**
    *   **Placement:** Massive Centered "X" (Shoulder-to-Opposite-Hip).
    *   **Ink:** Primary Red (Matte) + Primary Blue (Matte) + 3D Clear "Wet-Look" Topcoat on intersection only.
    *   **Garment:** Optic White 300 GSM Heavyweight.
*   **Gemini Studio Prompt:**

The chest features the 'Primary X-Lock' graphic: two massive intersecting diagonal bars in Primary Red and Primary Blue that form an 'X' from shoulders to hips. The ink has a raw, brushed-stroke texture. At the center sternum where the colors overlap, a high-gloss 3D clear 'wet-look' gel is applied. The 'ALPHANIX' wordmark is micro-printed in white within the blue bar on the lower-left rib. High-key lighting, 8k.`

## Design-088: "The Negative Lat-Void"
*   **Name:** The Negative Lat-Void
*   **Design Intent/Rationale:** Responds to the 2026 "Sculpting Seams" and "Minimalist" trends. This design uses "Negative Sculpting"—instead of printing a shape, it prints a massive "Void" of color around the natural muscle lines of the Latissimus Dorsi and Teres Major. This creates an optical illusion that makes the athlete's back look wider and more tapered. The brand name is hidden in the negative space of the "Void" edge.
*   **Technical Breakdown:**
    *   **Placement:** Lateral Back (Wrapping from Lats to Side-Ribs).
    *   **Ink:** "Electric Neon Green" (High-Opacity Matte) + "Black-Hole" Matte Black (to blend with garment).
    *   **Garment:** Void Black 240 GSM Luxury Oversized.
*   **Gemini Studio Prompt:**

The 'Negative Lat-Void' graphic uses Electric Neon Green ink to frame the lateral edges of the back and side-ribs, leaving the center lats as 'negative space' black fabric. The green ink has a sharp, laser-cut edge. The 'ALPHANIX' wordmark is vertically carved out of the neon green ink on the left rib, showing the black fabric underneath. Dramatic side-lighting to emphasize the 'V-taper' illusion, 8k.`
