# ALPHANIX Design Concepts: 2026-03-22 (AM)
Theme: **Chromatic Distortion & Macro-Anatomical Framing**

## Design-081: "The Prismatic Sternum-Flare"
*   **Name:** The Prismatic Sternum-Flare
*   **Design Intent/Rationale:** Responds to the 2026 "Vibrant Digital Print" and "Bold Aesthetics" trends by creating a high-energy focal point that radiates from the center-chest (sternum), framing the pectorals. It uses a "Prismatic Distortion" effect where the brand name is shattered into a multi-layered, semi-transparent stack, simulating a digital lens flare or a high-speed light burst.
*   **Technical Breakdown:**
    *   **Placement:** Center-Chest (Sternum) radiating outward to Mid-Pectorals.
    *   **Ink:** Transparent "Electric Cyan" + Transparent "Neon Magenta" (Overlapping to create Deep Violet) + 3D High-Gloss "Crystal" Gel.
    *   **Garment:** Optic White 300 GSM Heavyweight.
*   **Gemini Studio Prompt:**
    `A front-view professional studio photograph of an Optic White 300 GSM heavyweight t-shirt. The center-chest features a 'Prismatic Sternum-Flare' graphic where the word 'ALPHANIX' is shattered into overlapping transparent layers of Electric Cyan and Neon Magenta. Where the colors overlap, they create a vibrant Deep Violet. The entire graphic is coated in a 3D high-gloss 'Crystal' gel, giving it a refractive, glass-like appearance. High-key lighting, clean minimalist background, elite fitness streetwear aesthetic, 8k.`

## Design-082: "The Dorsal Kinetic-Rib"
*   **Name:** The Dorsal Kinetic-Rib
*   **Design Intent/Rationale:** Leverages the "Neo-Grunge" and "Oversized Silhouettes" trend of 2026 by using a massive, "heavy-weathered" graphic that tracks the body's natural rotation lines. Instead of a standard back print, this design wraps from the spine (Dorsal) around the right ribcage, creating a sense of motion even when the athlete is still. It uses "Solar Yellow" against a "Deep Earth Brown" for a high-contrast, industrial-nature fusion.
*   **Technical Breakdown:**
    *   **Placement:** Mid-Spine (Center-Back) wrapping aggressively around the Right Ribcage to Front-Right Abdominal.
    *   **Ink:** "Solar Yellow" (High-Opacity Matte) + "Heavy-Crackle" Weathering Agent.
    *   **Garment:** Deep Earth Brown 240 GSM Luxury Oversized.
*   **Gemini Studio Prompt:**
    `A rear-diagonal dynamic studio photograph of a Deep Earth Brown 240 GSM luxury oversized t-shirt. A massive 'Dorsal Kinetic-Rib' graphic in Solar Yellow wraps from the center-back spine all the way around the right side to the front-right rib. The yellow ink has a heavy-crackle weathered texture, looking like industrial paint on aged metal. The 'ALPHANIX' wordmark is vertically integrated into the crackle-lines along the side-body. High-contrast cinematic side-lighting, focus on the texture of the ink and the brown fabric, 8k.`
