# [26-03-26] Drop: Vertical Architecture & Chromatic Distortion

## Design-103: THE STRIATED MONOLITH-STRIPE
- **Vibe**: Elite verticality / Institutional Brutalism. Elongating, structural, and high-impact.
- **Placement**: Full Vertical Off-Center Stripe (Front Left: Shoulder to Hem) + Back-Right Shoulder "Stamp".
- **Ink**: Matte 'Institutional Grey' (Base) + 2mm High-Density 'Safety Orange' (Accents) on Void Black 240 GSM.
- **Gemini Prompt (Studio Shot)**: Front-diagonal professional studio photograph of a Void Black 240 GSM luxury oversized t-shirt. A massive vertical stripe in matte Institutional Grey runs from the left shoulder down to the bottom hem. 2mm high-density Safety Orange silicone accents are layered over the grey stripe in sharp, horizontal 'striations'. The ALPHANIX wordmark is vertically integrated into the orange accents. 8k.

## Design-104: THE PRISMATIC SEAM-FRACTURE
- **Vibe**: Digital refraction / Kinetic 'glitch' tailoring. Shimmering, technical, and edge-focused.
- **Placement**: Full Right Side-Seam (Armpit to Hem) + Right Sleeve Cuff (360-wrap).
- **Ink**: 'Electric Cyan' to 'Royal Violet' Split-Fountain + 'Reflective Micro-Glass' Additive on Cement Grey 300 GSM.
- **Gemini Prompt (Studio Shot)**: Side-profile dynamic studio photograph of a 'Cement Grey' 300 GSM heavyweight t-shirt. The 'Prismatic Seam-Fracture' graphic features a shimmering Electric Cyan to Royal Violet gradient running directly along the right side-seam. The ink contains 'Reflective Micro-Glass' that creates a prismatic shimmer under studio lights. ALPHANIX is deconstructed into 'shards' within the seam-line. 8k.
