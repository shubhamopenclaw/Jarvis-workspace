# ALPHANIX Design Concept: Design-107-260326
**Theme: The Bio-Digital Prism**

## Design-107: "The Bio-Digital Prism"
*   **Creative Director Rationale:** Directly implements the 2026 "Vibrant Digital Prints" and "High-Tech Performance" trends. This design moves away from the 3D-puff and structural-layering of recent drops (Design-101/105) into **Refractive Chromaticism**. It uses an asymmetrical "Prism-Fracture" that maps the left shoulder and chest, simulating light breaking through a digital lens. The use of "Electric Cobalt" and "Toxic Green" in a split-fountain gradient with a high-gloss "Crystal" topcoat creates an iridescent, shifting energy that captures the 2026 "Imaginative Styles" trend.

## Technical Breakdown
*   **Brand Identifier:** 'ALPHANIX' only (No technical blocks, no coordinates, no sector/ops references).
*   **Placement:** Asymmetrical Left Shoulder-to-Sternum "Fracture" (360-degree shoulder wrap + chest bleed).
*   **Ink Specification:** 
    *   **Base:** "Electric Cobalt" to "Toxic Green" Split-Fountain Gradient.
    *   **Texture:** High-Gloss "Crystal" Topcoat (Refractive/Iridescent).
    *   **Detail:** Micro-printed 'ALPHANIX' wordmark in reflective silver along the leading edge of the shards.
*   **Garment:** "Void Black" 240 GSM Luxury Oversized Fit.
