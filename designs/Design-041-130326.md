# ALPHANIX Design Concept: Design-041-130326
**Theme:** Primary Tonalism / Sculptural Silhouette

## Design-041: "The Monolith Seam"
*   **Name:** The Monolith Seam
*   **Design Intent/Rationale:** Tapping into the 2026 trend of "Primary Colors" (Red/Green) and "Contrast Seam Detailing." This design uses a bold Primary Red base with a tonal, high-gloss Red gel print. The graphic is not a standard logo but a series of thick, structural lines that mimic a "power harness" or skeletal frame, emphasizing the traps and lats. It’s "Quiet Luxury" for the gym—branding is only visible via the play of light on the gloss ink.
*   **Technical Breakdown:**
    *   **Placement:** Upper Back (Yoke) and Shoulders
    *   **Ink:** High-Gloss Transparent Red Gel (Tonal on Red fabric)
    *   **Garment:** Primary Red 300 GSM Heavyweight Boxy Fit
