# ALPHANIX Design Concept: "THE KINETIC WRAP" (Design-040)
**Date:** 12-03-26
**Aesthetic:** Fluid Motion / Digital Expressionism
**Trend Alignment:** Bold Colors / Dramatic Patterns (2026 Trend)

## Concept Overview
Embracing the 2026 trend of "bold colors" and "imaginative styles" moving away from neutrals. This design uses a "fluid wrap" technique where the brand name is treated like a ribbon of energy wrapping around the body, breaking the traditional boundaries of chest/back prints.

## Graphic Details
- **Text:** "ALPHANIX" in a fluid, elongated, "italicized-stretch" font.
- **Visual Style:** The letters are interconnected, looking like a single continuous brushstroke or a high-speed light trail.
- **Placement:** **Lower Left Front Hem wrapping around to the Lower Right Back.** (Spiral Wrap).
- **Ink / Technique:** **Neon Cobalt Blue Plastisol** on a **White** oversized tee.

## Technical Breakdown
- **Base Garment:** 240 GSM Premium Oversized Tee (Optic White).
- **Ink:** Neon Cobalt Blue Plastisol (Vibrant, flat finish).
- **Max Dimensions:** 24" Wrap-around length.
