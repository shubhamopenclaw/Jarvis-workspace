# ALPHANIX Design Concept - Design-036-110326-19-00

## Concept 1: "DISTORTED DISCIPLINE" (LOUD)
**Vibe:** Aggressive typographic architecture. High contrast. Urban technical.

### Technical Breakdown
- **Placement:** Large Center Chest (Spilling toward shoulders)
- **Max Dimensions:** 14" x 16"
- **Ink:** White Plastisol on Black Fabric.

### Graphics Description
The word "ALPHANIX" in a heavy, ultra-condensed bold sans-serif. The text is vertically sliced into three staggered segments, creating a "glitch" or "tectonic shift" effect. Overlaid on the bottom right of the main text is a small, high-density block of technical data: "OPS: T-DOT / SECTOR 6IX / VER: 2026.03.11".

### Prompt 1: The "Studio" Shot (Verification)
A hyper-realistic professional studio photograph of the ALPHANIX Primary Model Identity wearing a premium heavyweight oversized cotton t-shirt in Black colour. The model stands confidently against a soft off-white seamless studio background with subtle gradient shadow. High-end commercial fitness lighting with soft overhead key light and controlled rim light outlining the shoulders and traps. The graphic is a large center chest design featuring the word "ALPHANIX" in a heavy, ultra-condensed bold sans-serif, vertically sliced into three staggered segments creating a glitch effect. Small technical text "OPS: T-DOT / SECTOR 6IX" is printed in the bottom right corner of the graphic. Printed in thick white colour using matte screen-print ink. Allow only natural fabric folds. Shot on an 85mm lens, photorealistic, sharp focus, clean editorial campaign aesthetic, rugged luxury fitness brand.

### Prompt 2: The "Atmospheric" Shot (Vibe)
A cinematic editorial photograph of a premium heavyweight oversized t-shirt in Black colour hanging naturally from a matte black wooden hanger on a tree branch outdoors. The graphic is a large center chest design featuring the word "ALPHANIX" in a heavy, ultra-condensed bold sans-serif, vertically sliced into three staggered segments creating a tectonic shift effect, with technical data "OPS: T-DOT" printed in the corner. Printed with a matte white plastisol screen-printing ink. The Toronto skyline is visible in the background at sunrise, softly out of focus with shallow depth of field (85mm lens look). Early morning warm natural light casts subtle highlights and realistic shadows on the fabric. Slight breeze movement creates natural cotton drape. Photorealistic, high-end fashion campaign aesthetic, minimal color grading, rugged luxury mood.

## Concept 2: "MONOLITH MINIMAL" (QUIET)
**Vibe:** Sophisticated, "if you know, you know" luxury athleticism.

### Technical Breakdown
- **Placement:** Vertical Alignment along the left side seam (lower half).
- **Max Dimensions:** 3" x 10"
- **Ink:** Black-on-Black (High Gloss Puff) or Stealth Grey Plastisol.

### Graphics Description
A single, vertical line of geometric sans-serif text reading ".ALPHANIX / INSTITUTIONAL ATHLETICISM". The kerning is extremely wide (200%+). The font is thin but sharp. 

### Prompt 1: The "Studio" Shot (Verification)
A hyper-realistic professional studio photograph of the ALPHANIX Primary Model Identity wearing a premium heavyweight oversized cotton t-shirt in Black colour. The model stands confidently in a 3/4 side profile against a soft off-white seamless studio background. High-end commercial fitness lighting. A vertical graphic runs along the left side seam of the shirt reading ".ALPHANIX / INSTITUTIONAL ATHLETICISM" in a thin, wide-kerned geometric sans-serif. The print is a subtle stealth grey plastisol with a matte finish. Shot on an 85mm lens, photorealistic, sharp focus, clean editorial campaign aesthetic, rugged luxury fitness brand.

### Prompt 2: The "Atmospheric" Shot (Vibe)
A cinematic editorial photograph of a premium heavyweight oversized t-shirt in Black colour hanging naturally from a matte black wooden hanger on a tree branch outdoors. A vertical graphic runs along the side seam reading ".ALPHANIX / INSTITUTIONAL ATHLETICISM" in thin, widely spaced geometric typography. Printed with a matte stealth grey plastisol screen-printing ink. The Toronto skyline is visible in the background at sunrise, softly out of focus with shallow depth of field. Early morning warm natural light casts subtle highlights and realistic shadows on the fabric. Photorealistic, high-end fashion campaign aesthetic, minimal color grading, rugged luxury mood.
