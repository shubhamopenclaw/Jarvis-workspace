# Design-035-110326-03-00

## Concept 1: "FRAGMENTED DISCIPLINE" (LOUD)
**The Vibe:** High-impact typographic architecture. A large, bold "ALPHANIX" wordmark is sliced horizontally into three segments, with slight lateral offsets to create a "glitch" or "staggered" motion effect. In the negative space between the middle and bottom slices, "SECTOR 6IX" is printed in a tiny, clean geometric sans-serif for technical contrast.

### 1. Technical Breakdown
*   **Placement:** Center Chest (Oversized)
*   **Max Dimensions:** 12" x 10"
*   **Ink:** White Plastisol (High Opacity)

### 2. Prompt 1: The "Studio" Shot (Verification)
A hyper-realistic professional studio photograph of the ALPHANIX Primary Model Identity wearing a premium heavyweight oversized cotton t-shirt in Black colour. The model stands confidently against a soft off-white seamless studio background with subtle gradient shadow. High-end commercial fitness lighting with soft overhead key light and controlled rim light outlining the shoulders and traps. The graphic is a large bold 'ALPHANIX' wordmark horizontally sliced into three staggered segments with a slight lateral offset; in the gap between the lower slices, the text 'SECTOR 6IX' is printed in tiny, ultra-clean geometric sans-serif. The design is printed in thick white colour using matte screen-print ink. Allow only natural fabric folds. Shot on an 85mm lens, photorealistic, sharp focus, clean editorial campaign aesthetic, rugged luxury fitness brand.

### 3. Prompt 2: The "Atmospheric" Shot (Vibe)
A cinematic editorial photograph of a premium heavyweight oversized t-shirt in Black colour hanging naturally from a matte black wooden hanger on a tree branch outdoors. The graphic features a large bold 'ALPHANIX' wordmark horizontally sliced into three staggered segments with a slight lateral offset; in the gap between the lower slices, the text 'SECTOR 6IX' is printed in tiny, ultra-clean geometric sans-serif. The design is printed with a matte white plastisol screen-printing ink. The Toronto skyline is visible in the background at sunrise, softly out of focus with shallow depth of field (85mm lens look). Early morning warm natural light casts subtle highlights and realistic shadows on the fabric. Slight breeze movement creates natural cotton drape. Photorealistic, high-end fashion campaign aesthetic, minimal color grading, rugged luxury mood.

## Concept 2: "THE SILENT ARCHITECT" (QUIET)
**The Vibe:** Minimalist, "Quiet Luxury" placement. No chest print. A vertical, narrow column of text runs down the upper spine. The word "ALPHANIX" is oriented vertically (90-degree rotation), with "EST. 2026 / TORONTO" printed in a much smaller, secondary font justified to the bottom of the "X".

### 1. Technical Breakdown
*   **Placement:** Back Upper Spine (Nape to Mid-Blade)
*   **Max Dimensions:** 3" x 12"
*   **Ink:** Grey Plastisol (Low Contrast/Tonal)

### 2. Prompt 1: The "Studio" Shot (Verification)

He is wearing a premium heavyweight oversized cotton t-shirt in Black colour. The model stands confidently against a soft off-white seamless studio background with subtle gradient shadow. The graphic is a minimalist vertical 'ALPHANIX' wordmark running down the spine, with 'EST. 2026 / TORONTO' in tiny text justified to the bottom, printed in a subtle matte grey colour using screen-print ink. Allow only natural fabric folds. Shot on an 85mm lens, photorealistic, sharp focus, clean editorial campaign aesthetic, rugged luxury fitness brand.
