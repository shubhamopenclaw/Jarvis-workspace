# ALPHANIX Design Concept: Design-065-180326
Theme: **Primary Contrast Piping & Anatomical Compression Illusion**

## Design-065: "The Primary Seam-Sculpt"
*   **Name:** The Primary Seam-Sculpt
*   **Design Intent/Rationale:** Directly targets the 2026 "contrast piping and seam detailing" trend identified in research. Instead of standard graphics, this design uses high-density "Electric Cyan" ink to simulate anatomical compression seams. It creates a "harness" effect that visually broadens the shoulders and tapers the waist, aligning with the "sculpt and flatter" fitness aesthetic. 
*   **Technical Breakdown:**
    *   **Placement:** Front "Harness" (Shoulder-to-Sternum) + Side-Rib "Vents"
    *   **Ink:** High-Density "Electric Cyan" Silicone (3D Raised Texture)
    *   **Garment:** Matte Black 300 GSM Heavyweight Cotton
*   **Gemini Studio Prompt:**
    `A hyper-realistic professional studio photograph of a premium 300 GSM Matte Black heavyweight t-shirt. The shirt features "Electric Cyan" high-density silicone lines that map the front anatomical structure, running from the mid-shoulders down to a central "sternum lock" point. Parallel "ventilation" lines run along the side-ribs. The word "ALPHANIX" is micro-embossed into the thickest part of the cyan silicone on the sternum. The ink has a raised, rubberized 3D texture that catches sharp studio highlights. Cinematic lighting, elite technical fitness streetwear aesthetic, 8k.`
*   **Atmospheric Prompt:**
    `A moody, high-end editorial shot of the "Primary Seam-Sculpt" t-shirt in Matte Black draped over a brushed steel weight rack in a minimalist, dimly lit Toronto garage gym. The Electric Cyan 3D lines glow under a single overhead cool-white LED. 50mm lens, photorealistic, "Institutional" discipline vibe, sleek and technical.`

# ALPHANIX Design Concept: Design-066-180326
Theme: **Y2K Co-ord Layering & Primary Chromaticism**

## Design-066: "The Y2K Cobalt-Block"
*   **Name:** The Y2K Cobalt-Block
*   **Design Intent/Rationale:** Leverages the 2026 trend of "Primary Colors" (Cobalt Blue) and "Layered Y2K tops." It uses a massive, solid block of ink at the top of the garment to create the visual illusion of a layered co-ord or a technical "shrug" worn over a white base. This breaks the traditional "logo-on-chest" mold entirely.
*   **Technical Breakdown:**
    *   **Placement:** Upper Third "Shoulder-Cap" (Collar to Mid-Bicep Wrap)
    *   **Ink:** Matte Primary Cobalt Blue (Solid Block) + High-Gloss White Typography
    *   **Garment:** Optic White 240 GSM Oversized Cotton
*   **Gemini Studio Prompt:**
    `A high-resolution product shot of an oversized Optic White 240 GSM t-shirt. The upper third of the garment—including the collar, shoulders, and upper sleeves—is covered in a solid, flat-matte Primary Cobalt Blue screen print, creating a "layered shrug" effect. The word "ALPHANIX" is printed across the back-shoulders in massive, high-gloss white typography that reflects the light. Clean studio background, minimalist Y2K streetwear vibe, sharp focus on the crisp line between blue ink and white fabric, 8k.`
*   **Atmospheric Prompt:**
    `A cinematic photograph of the "Y2K Cobalt-Block" t-shirt hanging from a minimalist black wire hanger against a stark white gallery wall in Toronto. The Primary Cobalt Blue block is punchy and vibrant against the white fabric. Bright, diffused daylight from a large window creates soft shadows. Photorealistic, high-end boutique aesthetic, futuristic and clean mood.`
