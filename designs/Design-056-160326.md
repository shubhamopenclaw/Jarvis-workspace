## Design-056: "The Y2K Layer-Block"
*   **Name:** The Y2K Layer-Block
*   **Design Intent/Rationale:** Inspired by the 2026 trend for "layered Y2K tops" and "vibrant color blocking." This design creates a visual illusion of a layered t-shirt (co-ord style) by using a massive block of ink across the bottom half, contrasting with a primary color base.
*   **Technical Breakdown:**
    *   **Placement:** Bottom 40% Body Wrap (Full circle)
    *   **Ink:** Matte Forest Green (Base Block) + High-Gloss Black Typography
    *   **Garment:** Optic White 240 GSM
*   **Gemini Studio Prompt:**
    `A high-resolution product shot of an oversized optic white t-shirt. The bottom 40% of the garment is completely covered in a solid, matte Forest Green screen print, creating a 'layered' visual effect. Overlapping this green block is the word 'ALPHANIX' in massive, high-gloss black typography that spans the entire width and bleeds off the side seams. The contrast between the matte green and the shiny black ink is highly visible. Clean studio background, minimal Y2K streetwear vibe, 8k.`
