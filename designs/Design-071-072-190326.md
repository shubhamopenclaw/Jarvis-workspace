# ALPHANIX Design Concept: Design-071-190326
## Concept: THE NEON NERVE-MAP (Digital Kineticism)

### Vibe
Hyper-digital, anatomical, and high-energy. This design leverages the 2026 "Bold Aesthetics" trend (neon colors, digital prints) but applies it with technical precision. It mimics a "nerve map" or fiber-optic wiring that tracks the body's kinetic flow.

### Placement
**The "Scapula-to-Sternum" Circuit.** 
The graphic starts at the right shoulder blade (back), wraps over the trapezius, and terminates at the center sternum (front). This creates a continuous "flow" that highlights the athlete's frame from both front and back angles.

### Ink & Texture
- **Ink 1:** Neon "Electric Magenta" (Solid Matte Base).
- **Ink 2:** "Liquid Chrome" Silver (Ultra-fine pinstripes running through the magenta).
- **Texture:** High-Density (3D Raised) silicone on the magenta base to give it a "cable" feel.

### Garment
- **Color:** Deep Obsidian (Matte Black).
- **Weight:** 300 GSM Heavyweight Boxy Fit.

### Studio Prompt (Gemini)
`A hyper-realistic professional studio photograph of a premium 300 GSM Deep Obsidian (Matte Black) heavyweight t-shirt. An asymmetrical 'Neon Nerve-Map' graphic in Neon Electric Magenta wraps from the right scapula, over the shoulder, and down to the center sternum. The graphic features ultra-fine 'Liquid Chrome' silver pinstripes running through the raised magenta silicone lines, simulating fiber-optic wiring. The 'ALPHANIX' wordmark is micro-embossed into the magenta cable at the collarbone. High-contrast cinematic lighting, focus on the 3D texture and vibrant neon against the matte black fabric. 8k.`

# ALPHANIX Design Concept: Design-072-190326
## Concept: THE GLITCH-BLOCK MONOLITH (Y2K Futurism)

### Vibe
Rebellious, oversized, and structurally disruptive. It combines the 2026 "Baggy Rebellion" trend with a digital "glitch" aesthetic. It moves away from centered graphics to a "corner-heavy" weight that shifts the silhouette's visual balance.

### Placement
**Lower-Right Hem "Corner-Weight".**
A massive, rectangular block of color that starts 2 inches from the bottom-right hem and wraps around to the back-right, "grounding" the oversized fit.

### Ink & Texture
- **Ink 1:** Primary "Acid Green" (Neon Green).
- **Ink 2:** Transparent "Void Black" (Used to create a 'digital glitch' or 'scan-line' effect over the green).
- **Texture:** Heavy-Crackle Plastisol on the Acid Green to give it a weathered, "analog-to-digital" feel.

### Garment
- **Color:** Cement Grey.
- **Weight:** 240 GSM Luxury Oversized Fit.

### Studio Prompt (Gemini)
`A low-angle product shot of a Cement Grey 240 GSM luxury oversized t-shirt. The design features a massive 'Glitch-Block Monolith' on the lower-right hem, wrapping around to the back-right hip. The block is printed in a vibrant Primary 'Acid Green' with a heavy-crackle weathered texture. Superimposed over the green are transparent 'Void Black' scan-lines, creating a digital glitch effect. The 'ALPHANIX' wordmark is printed in high-gloss black, vertically aligned within the green block. Raw urban streetwear aesthetic, industrial studio setting, sharp shadows. 8k.`
