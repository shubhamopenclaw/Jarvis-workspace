## Concept 1: LOUD — "SECTOR 6IX VERTICAL"
**Aesthetic:** Industrial Dominance. High-impact typography that commands space.
**Description:**
A massive, vertical typographic stack running down the spine of the shirt. The word "ALPHANIX" is rotated 90 degrees, running from the neck down to the mid-back in a heavy, condensed sans-serif font (Bebas Neue style). Intersecting this at the top (near the neck) is "SECTOR 6IX" in a smaller, horizontal eroded stencil font, creating a cross-hair effect. The ink has a subtle "cracked concrete" texture.

**Rationale:**
Leverages the "Impact Family" directive. The vertical orientation elongates the wearer's silhouette (gym aesthetic), while the "Sector 6ix" adds the required Toronto technical code without being a souvenir.

### Technical Breakdown
*   **Placement:** Center Back (Vertical Spine)
*   **Max Dimensions:** 12" x 18"
*   **Ink:** White Plastisol (Heavy Deposit) with Distressed Texture additive.
