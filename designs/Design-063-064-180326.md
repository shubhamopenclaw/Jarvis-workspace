# ALPHANIX Design Concept: 2026-03-18 (PM)
Theme: **Shattered Kineticism & Chromatic Seam-Lock**

## Design-063: "The Kinetic Fracture-Hem"
*   **Name:** The Kinetic Fracture-Hem
*   **Design Intent/Rationale:** Targets the 2026 "Bold Aesthetics" and "Dynamic Movement" trends. Instead of a standard chest or back print, this design focuses on the **lower kinetic chain**. A "shattered" geometric blast of the brand name originates from the front-left hem and wraps aggressively around the hip to the lower back. It uses a "Split-Fountain" neon gradient to simulate digital energy.
*   **Technical Breakdown:**
    *   **Placement:** Lower Left Hem to Back-Right Wrap (Asymmetrical Blast)
    *   **Ink:** Neon Citron to Electric Lime Gradient (Split-Fountain) + High-Density Matte Black Outline
    *   **Garment:** Heavyweight Matte Black (300 GSM)
*   **Gemini Studio Prompt:**
    `A hyper-realistic professional studio photograph of a premium 300 GSM Matte Black heavyweight t-shirt. A massive "shattered" graphic in a Neon Citron to Electric Lime gradient explodes from the front-left bottom hem, wrapping aggressively around the side to the lower back. The word "ALPHANIX" is deconstructed into sharp, kinetic shards within the blast. Each shard has a subtle high-density matte black 3D outline. Low-angle shot, cinematic lighting highlighting the neon ink's vibrancy against the black fabric, futuristic elite fitness streetwear, 8k.`

## Design-064: "The Chromatic Seam-Lock"
*   **Name:** The Chromatic Seam-Lock
*   **Design Intent/Rationale:** Leans into the 2026 "Contrast Piping" and "Anatomical Scuplting" trend without using physical fabric piping. It uses a 3D "Liquid Gel" ink to create a structural "harness" that locks the shoulder and follows the traps. The brand name is micro-printed inside the "gel-track," creating a high-tech, lab-issued aesthetic.
*   **Technical Breakdown:**
    *   **Placement:** Dual Shoulder-to-Nape "Harness" (Upper Traps & Delts)
    *   **Ink:** Translucent "Cyber-Red" 3D Gel + White Micro-Typography
    *   **Garment:** Gunmetal Grey (240 GSM)
*   **Gemini Studio Prompt:**
    `A high-resolution product shot from a high-angle rear-diagonal of a premium Gunmetal Grey 240 GSM t-shirt. A 3D structural "harness" graphic in translucent Cyber-Red liquid gel tracks the natural lines of the upper traps and wraps over the shoulders. The word "ALPHANIX" is micro-printed in crisp white technical typography inside the red gel track at the nape of the neck. The gel has a high-gloss, wet-look finish that catches sharp studio highlights. Professional fitness lookbook style, technical gear aesthetic, 8k.`
