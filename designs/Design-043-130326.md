# ALPHANIX Design Concept: Design-043-130326
Theme: **Thermal Displacement / Primary Heat**

## Design-043: "The Thermal Gradient"
*   **Name:** The Thermal Gradient
*   **Design Intent/Rationale:** Inspired by the 2026 "Bold Colors & Dramatic Patterns" trend. It uses the ALPHANIX name as a thermal heat map, signifying the heat generated during elite performance. This breaks away from structural lines into organic, fluid gradients.
*   **Technical Breakdown:**
    *   **Placement:** Chest to Side-Rib Wrap (Asymmetrical)
    *   **Ink:** Tri-tone Split Fountain (Vibrant Red -> Burnt Orange -> Deep Purple)
    *   **Garment:** Matte Black 300 GSM Heavyweight
*   **Gemini Studio Prompt:**
    `A hyper-realistic professional studio photograph of the ALPHANIX Primary Model Identity wearing a premium 300 GSM heavyweight boxy t-shirt in Matte Black. The shirt features an asymmetrical 'thermal heat map' graphic wrapping from the center chest around to the right ribs. The word 'ALPHANIX' is distorted within the heat map, rendered in a smooth gradient of Vibrant Red, Burnt Orange, and Deep Purple. The ink has a soft-hand plastisol finish. 85mm lens, high-end editorial fitness aesthetic, moody lighting focusing on the color transition.`
