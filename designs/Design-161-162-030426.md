# Design Concept 161: THE CHROMATIC-SUTURE MANTLE
**Date:** 03-04-26
**Theme:** 2026 Kinetic Sculpting / Primary Contrast
**Inspiration:** 2026 Trends focusing on "contrast piping" and "sculptural seam detailing" that flatter athletic anatomy, combined with the return of bold primary colors.

### Graphic Description
- **Front:** A vertical, slightly curved "suture" line (high-density 3D puff print) in **Vibrant Cobalt Blue** that starts at the left collarbone, arcs down across the pectoral, and disappears into the right side-seam.
- **Brand Placement:** The 'ALPHANIX' wordmark is printed in a micro-scale (12pt font height) in **Reflective Silver** exactly parallel to the curve of the suture line, positioned near the solar plexus.
- **Back:** The cobalt suture line reappears at the right shoulder blade, cutting diagonally across the back to the left hip.
- **Colorway:** Matte Black Heavyweight Oversized Tee.

# Design Concept 162: THE NEON-FLUX VESTIGE
**Date:** 03-04-26
**Theme:** 2026 Digital-Vibrant Urbanism
**Inspiration:** 2026 "Gym-to-Street" evolution emphasizing imaginative digital prints and "Neon Primary" accents.

### Graphic Description
- **Placement:** Extreme side-body placement.
- **Graphic:** A "shattered" digital gradient effect that looks like light trailing behind a moving object. It uses a **Electric Lime-to-Neon Red** gradient. The graphic starts at the bottom hem of the left side and "climbs" up toward the armpit in a series of abstract, geometric shards.
- **Brand Placement:** 'ALPHANIX' is vertically aligned on the *back* of the right sleeve, printed in a **Deep Charcoal Matte** (tone-on-tone effect) so it only catches the light at certain angles.
- **Colorway:** Charcoal Grey Boxy Tee.
