# Design-169: THE FLORAL-DECAY CHASSIS
- **Vibe**: 2026 Neo-Romantic Streetwear / Organic Tech Fusion. Contrast between delicate floral patterns and aggressive industrial framing.
- **Placement**: Massive Asymmetrical Wrap (Left Hip, sweeping across Abdomen to Right Shoulder).
- **Ink**: Matte Dusty Rose (Floral Base) + High-Density "Security Black" (3D Industrial Frame) + "Crystal" Clear Gloss (Highlight).
- **Gemini Prompt (Studio Shot)**: Low-angle dynamic studio photograph of a 'Void Black' 240 GSM luxury oversized t-shirt. A massive 'FLORAL-DECAY CHASSIS' graphic sweeps from the left hip to the right shoulder. Intricate, faded 'Dusty Rose' floral patterns are caged inside a raised 3mm 'Security Black' high-density silicone geometric frame. 'ALPHANIX' is micro-printed in high-gloss 'Crystal' clear gel along the frame's edge. 8k.

# Design-170: THE PIPED-GINGHAM MANTLE
- **Vibe**: 2026 Retro-Modernist Athleisure / "2016-Core" Revival. Sophisticated use of traditional patterns (Gingham) with futuristic athletic detailing (Contrast Piping).
- **Placement**: Full Upper-Mantle (Shoulder-to-Shoulder) + Symmetrical "Double-Pipe" Lateral Vents.
- **Ink**: Cobalt Blue Gingham (Digital Sublimation/Screen-Print) + Neon "Electric Mint" (3D Silicone Piping).
- **Gemini Prompt (Studio Shot)**: High-angle rear-view minimalist studio photograph of a 'Bone' Off-White 300 GSM heavyweight boxy t-shirt. The upper mantle area (shoulders and nape) is covered in a 'Cobalt Blue' gingham pattern. Raised 3mm 'Electric Mint' neon silicone piping maps the natural curves of the traps and lats, intersecting the gingham. 'ALPHANIX' is carved out as negative space in the piping at the nape. 8k.
