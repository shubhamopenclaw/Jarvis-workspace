# ALPHANIX Design Concepts - 040426

## Design 167: THE VINTAGE VESTIGE MANTLE
**Aesthetic:** Vintage Activewear x Grunge Goth Fusion (2026 Trend)
**Color Palette:** Faded Charcoal, Dust Rose, Bone White.
**Graphic Placements:**
- **Front:** A faded, oversized 'ALPHANIX' in a 90s-style varsity font, distressed to look like a cracked screen print from decades ago.
- **Back:** A large, central "Goth-Butterfly" motif—an anatomical ribcage forming the wings, with 'ALPHANIX' subtly integrated into the spine. The butterfly is rendered in a "Grunge-Goth" hand-sketched style with digital ink splatters.
- **Sleeve (Right):** A single "washed-out" barcode-style stripe near the cuff, but without technical data—just aesthetic texture.
**Concept:** This design leans into the 2026 "cool girl/creator" trend of mixing vintage activewear shapes with dark, feminine-leaning grunge elements. It avoids all technical blocks and focuses on the "worn-in" organic feel of a legacy brand.

## Design 168: THE NEON-FLARE KINETIC
**Aesthetic:** High-Tech Performance x Digital Neon (2026 Trend)
**Color Palette:** Electric Cobalt, Neon Lime, Deep Obsidian.
**Graphic Placements:**
- **Front:** Minimalist. A small, high-density 'ALPHANIX' logo in Neon Lime placed on the lower-left hem rather than the chest.
- **Side Panels (Both):** Vibrant digital prints that simulate "motion blur" in Electric Cobalt and Neon Lime. These prints wrap from the back ribs around to the front oblique, following the natural torque of a human torso in motion.
- **Back:** A vertical "ALPHANIX" running down the center-spine in a clean, sans-serif font, printed in a reflective glass-bead ink that only pops under direct light.
**Concept:** This design targets the "Urban Athletic" 2026 trend. It moves away from centered chest pieces, using the side-body for high-velocity digital prints to create a silhouette that feels fast even when standing still. No coordinates or technical jargon—just pure kinetic energy.
