# ALPHANIX Design Concept: "THE ARCHITECTURAL RIB" (Design-039)
**Date:** 12-03-26
**Aesthetic:** Structural Minimalist / Brutalist
**Trend Alignment:** Contrast Piping / Seam Detailing (2026 Trend)

## Concept Overview
Inspired by the "contrast piping" and "seam detailing" trend, this design uses the ALPHANIX brand name to create a structural ribbing effect. Instead of traditional piping, the letters are stacked vertically to form a "spine" or "rib" that reinforces the silhouette of the garment.

## Graphic Details
- **Text:** "ALPHANIX" stacked vertically, with letters rotated 90 degrees.
- **Visual Style:** Ultra-clean, high-kerning, technical sans-serif.
- **Placement:** Vertical along the **Back Spine** (Center Back), running from the neck line down to the mid-back.
- **Ink / Technique:** **Matte Black Silicone** on a **Charcoal Grey** shirt (Tonal/Reflective). The silicone gives a raised, structural rubberized feel.

## Technical Breakdown
- **Base Garment:** 300 GSM Heavyweight Boxy Tee (Charcoal Grey).
- **Ink:** Matte Black High-Density Silicone.
- **Max Dimensions:** 3" Wide x 18" Long.
