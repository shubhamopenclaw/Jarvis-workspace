# Design-031: ERODED ARCHITECTURE & LATENT GEOMETRY

## Concept 1: ERODED ARCHITECTURE (LOUD)
**Vibe:** Institutional Athleticism meets Industrial Decay. High impact, technical.

### Technical Breakdown
- **Placement:** Full Back (Primary), Center Chest (Secondary)
- **Max Dimensions:** 14" x 18" (Back), 4" x 1" (Chest)
- **Ink:** White Plastisol with intentional "distress" texture in the screen.
- **Typography:** Bebas Neue (Impact) for "ALPHANIX", Montserrat (Technical) for data.

### Prompt 1: The "Studio" Shot (Verification)
A hyper-realistic professional studio photograph of the ALPHANIX Primary Model Identity wearing a premium heavyweight oversized cotton t-shirt in Black colour. The model stands with his back to the camera, head turned slightly in profile, confidently against a soft off-white seamless studio background with subtle gradient shadow. High-end commercial fitness lighting with soft overhead key light and controlled rim light outlining the shoulders and traps. The back graphic features the word "ALPHANIX" in large, bold, condensed Bebas Neue typography with a realistic eroded wireframe texture, printed in thick white matte screen-print ink. Below it, small technical text reads "OPS: T-DOT // SECTOR 6IX" in a clean staggered grid. Allow only natural fabric folds. Shot on an 85mm lens, photorealistic, sharp focus, clean editorial campaign aesthetic, rugged luxury fitness brand.

### Prompt 2: The "Atmospheric" Shot (Vibe)
A cinematic editorial photograph of a premium heavyweight oversized t-shirt in Black colour hanging naturally from a matte black wooden hanger on a tree branch outdoors. The back graphic, featuring a large white eroded "ALPHANIX" logo with technical data "OPS: T-DOT", is printed with a matte white plastisol screen-printing ink. The Toronto skyline is visible in the background at sunrise, softly out of focus with shallow depth of field (85mm lens look). Early morning warm natural light casts subtle highlights and realistic shadows on the fabric. Slight breeze movement creates natural cotton drape. Photorealistic, high-end fashion campaign aesthetic, minimal color grading, rugged luxury mood.

## Concept 2: LATENT GEOMETRY (QUIET)
**Vibe:** Minimalist, "Quiet Luxury," technical precision.

### Technical Breakdown
- **Placement:** Left Collarbone (Typography), Lower Right Hip (Geometric)
- **Max Dimensions:** 3" x 0.5" (Collarbone), 2" x 2" (Hip)
- **Ink:** White Plastisol (Thin, high-mesh count for sharp detail).
- **Typography:** Montserrat (Geometric Sans).

### Prompt 1: The "Studio" Shot (Verification)
A hyper-realistic professional studio photograph of the ALPHANIX Primary Model Identity wearing a premium heavyweight oversized cotton t-shirt in Black colour. The model stands confidently in a 3/4 view against a soft off-white seamless studio background with subtle gradient shadow. High-end commercial fitness lighting with soft overhead key light and controlled rim light outlining the chest and shoulders. A small, elegant "ALPHANIX" logo in widely-spaced white Montserrat typography is printed on the left collarbone. On the lower right hip, a single sharp 45-degree geometric white line is visible. Printed in thick white colour using matte screen-print ink. Allow only natural fabric folds. Shot on an 85mm lens, photorealistic, sharp focus, clean editorial campaign aesthetic, rugged luxury fitness brand.

### Prompt 2: The "Atmospheric" Shot (Vibe)
A cinematic editorial photograph of a premium heavyweight oversized t-shirt in Black colour hanging naturally from a matte black wooden hanger on a tree branch outdoors. The minimal white "ALPHANIX" collarbone typography and a sharp geometric line on the hip are printed with a matte white plastisol screen-printing ink. The Toronto skyline is visible in the background at sunrise, softly out of focus with shallow depth of field (85mm lens look). Early morning warm natural light casts subtle highlights and realistic shadows on the fabric. Slight breeze movement creates natural cotton drape. Photorealistic, high-end fashion campaign aesthetic, minimal color grading, rugged luxury mood.
