# ALPHANIX Design Concepts: 2026-03-19
Theme: **Thermal Topography & Prism-Block Co-ords**

## Design-067: "The Thermal Topograph"
*   **Name:** The Thermal Topograph
*   **Design Intent/Rationale:** Captures the 2026 trend of "bold colors" and "imaginative styles" by using a topographical heat-map aesthetic. It simulates the thermal energy of a high-intensity workout. The placement is a "Chest-to-Shoulder Overflow," breaking the standard chest-box by bleeding into the shoulder seam.
*   **Technical Breakdown:**
    *   **Placement:** Upper Left Chest to Left Shoulder Wrap (Bleeding over the seam)
    *   **Ink:** Tri-tone Split Fountain (Neon Red / Electric Orange / Solar Yellow) + Matte Clear "Sand-Grain" Texture Overlay
    *   **Garment:** Charcoal Grey 300 GSM Heavyweight
*   **Gemini Studio Prompt:**
    `A hyper-realistic professional studio photograph of a premium 300 GSM Charcoal Grey heavyweight t-shirt. The shirt features a "Thermal Topograph" graphic—a series of organic, undulating topographical lines in a vibrant Neon Red to Solar Yellow gradient. The graphic starts at the center chest and "overflows" aggressively over the left shoulder seam onto the upper sleeve. The word "ALPHANIX" is embedded into the thickest thermal line on the chest. The ink has a unique matte "sand-grain" texture that diffuses the light. Cinematic side-lighting, 8k, futuristic fitness streetwear.`

## Design-068: "The Prism-Block Pillar"
*   **Name:** The Prism-Block Pillar
*   **Design Intent/Rationale:** Targets the 2026 "Primary Color" and "Y2K Layered Co-ord" trends. It uses a massive vertical "Pillar" placement on the side of the body, creating a structural look that elongates the torso. It features "Prismatic Transparency"—overlapping primary colors (Blue and Yellow) to create a third color (Green) where they intersect, giving a technical, digital feel.
*   **Technical Breakdown:**
    *   **Placement:** Vertical Right Side-Body (Armpit to Hem)
    *   **Ink:** Transparent Primary Blue + Transparent Primary Yellow (Overlapping to create Green)
    *   **Garment:** Optic White 240 GSM
*   **Gemini Studio Prompt:**
    `A high-resolution product shot of an Optic White 240 GSM oversized t-shirt, viewed from a side-profile. A massive vertical "Prism-Block" graphic runs down the right side from the armpit to the bottom hem. The graphic consists of two overlapping transparent rectangular pillars in Primary Blue and Primary Yellow; where they overlap, a vibrant Green is formed. The word "ALPHANIX" is printed vertically in high-gloss black typography inside the blue pillar. Sharp, clean studio lighting, high-contrast digital streetwear aesthetic, 8k.`
