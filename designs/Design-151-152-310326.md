# ALPHANIX Design Engine - [31-03-26] Drop: Digital Graffiti & Kinetic Primary-Clash

## Design-151: THE NEON-SCRIBBLE CHASSIS
- **Concept**: 2026 "Gym-to-Street" Expressive Technicality. This design rejects the "clean" tech look for a raw, hand-drawn digital aesthetic. It mimics the energy of a graffiti artist tagging high-performance gear.
- **Vibe**: High-Energy / Expressive / Technical-Chaos.
- **Placement**: Massive Asymmetrical 270-degree Wrap. The graphic explodes from the Left Shoulder-Cap, sweeps across the Upper Chest, and bleeds down the Right Side-Rib to the Hem.
- **Ink Strategy**: 
    - **Base**: Electric Cobalt (Matte High-Opacity).
    - **Overlayer**: Neon Toxic Green (Hand-Drawn "Scribble" Halftone).
    - **Detail**: 3D High-Gloss "Crystal" Gel on the 'ALPHANIX' typography within the scribble.
- **Gemini Prompt (Studio Shot)**: Front-diagonal dynamic studio photograph of a 'Void Black' 240 GSM luxury oversized t-shirt. A massive, aggressive 'NEON-SCRIBBLE CHASSIS' graphic in Electric Cobalt and Neon Toxic Green sweeps from the left shoulder to the right hem. The green ink has a 'hand-drawn' spray-paint texture. A high-gloss 'Crystal' gel is layered over the 'ALPHANIX' wordmark which is distorted into the scribble. 8k resolution, high-contrast lighting.

## Design-152: THE PRIMARY KINETIC-SLING
- **Concept**: 2026 "Primary-Pop" Athleticism. Utilizing bold, clashing primary colors to create a sense of high-velocity movement. It's a modern take on 90s athletic "slings" but with a futuristic, digital edge.
- **Vibe**: High-Impact / Velocity / Modern-Vintage.
- **Placement**: Massive Continuous Diagonal "Sling". Starts at the Back-Right Hem, wraps over the Left Shoulder, and finishes at the Front-Right Hip.
- **Ink Strategy**:
    - **Primary 1**: Lava Red (Heavy-Crackle Weathered).
    - **Primary 2**: Deep Cobalt Blue (Solid Matte).
    - **Accent**: Optic White (3D Micro-Typography 'ALPHANIX' scattered along the edges).
- **Gemini Prompt (Studio Shot)**: Front-view dynamic studio photograph of an 'Optic White' 300 GSM heavyweight t-shirt. A massive, continuous diagonal 'PRIMARY KINETIC-SLING' in Lava Red (Heavy-Crackle texture) and Deep Cobalt Blue (Matte) wraps from the back-right hem, over the left shoulder, to the front-right hip. Fine 'ALPHANIX' micro-typography is printed in 3D white ink along the seam-lines of the graphic. 8k resolution, dynamic action pose.
