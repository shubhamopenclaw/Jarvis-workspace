# ALPHANIX Design Concept: THE KINETIC SHELL
**ID:** Design-057-170326
**Theme:** Utility-Tech & Structural Tension

## Design Intent / Rationale
Directly addressing the 2026 "Tech Shell" trend. This design moves away from standard center-chest/back placements and instead uses a "Shell" placement—wrapping around the outer deltoids and upper back. It simulates the structural bracing of a technical harness or a performance exoshell. It uses the "Vintage/Tech Contrast" by pairing a heavy matte fabric with a high-vis primary blue ink.

## Technical Breakdown
- **Placement:** Outer Deltoid to Outer Deltoid (Wrapping across the upper back yoke)
- **Ink:** Matte Primary Blue (Cobalt) + Clear 3D Silicone Gloss (for a 'wet' structural rim)
- **Garment:** Deep Charcoal Grey 300 GSM (Heavyweight Boxy Fit)
- **Typography:** ALPHANIX wordmark is split—'ALPHA' on the left shoulder shell, 'NIX' on the right, connected by a thick structural "tension line" across the back.
